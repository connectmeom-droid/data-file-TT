About the Institute


Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Ranchi was founded by the industrialist and philanthropist Late Shri B.M. Birla in 1955. Birla Institute of Technology Mesra; Off Campus Patna came into existence in the year 2006 along with the initiative of Government of Bihar with a vision to provide quality education in the students of Bihar who wishes to pursue their career in technical education. By far, BITP is one of the best equipped, technically sound professional colleges in the state of Bihar. It has fully networked campus. The infrastructure includes 25 Acres of academic space including 1 Girl's hostel and 03 Boy's Hostel with the capacity of approximately 300 each (1200 Approx.). BIT Patna is committed to provide state-of-the-art facilities to its students.

Currently Institute is offering following programmes:
  1. Bachelor of Technology Degree in Computer Science & Engineering
  2. Bachelor of Technology Degree in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  3. Bachelor of Technology Degree in Electronics & Communications Engineering
  4. Bachelor of Technology Degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  5. Bachelor of Technology Degree in Mechanical Engineering
  6. Bachelor of Technology Degree in Civil Engineering
  7. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  8. Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  9. Masters of Business Applications (MBA)
  10. Masters of Technology (M.Tech.) in CSE
  11. Masters of Technology (M.Tech.) in ECE (Wireless Communications)
  12. Integrated M.Sc. in Mathematics and Computing
  13. Ph.D. Programme in Science, Engineering and Management.
BITP seeks to attract the best students from diverse backgrounds and offer them an excellent educational experience. It offers more flexibility and choice to the students in the academic programmes, an inviting and stimulating ambience for education and research, a rich suite of extra and co-curricular activities, personality development, life-skills and career planning will be a part of the holistic development.

The Campus strives to achieve excellence in Teaching, Research and Consultancy by creating an environment in which the faculty and students share a passion for creating, sharing and applying knowledge to continuously improve the quality of education. To keep abreast with latest pedagogy the Institute organizes various International and National Seminar/conferences/ workshops/ FDP/ Short term courses/ Guest lecture etc. from time to time.

Academic Structure

Institute follows Choice-Based Credit System (CBCS) course structure, which provides opportunity to opt choice-based electives along with the course subject.

There is also provision of completing minor courses of other branch along with the subjects of main branch.

Rules For Change Branch

There is a provision for branch change (B.Tech. 2nd year students (3rd semester)) subject to availability of seats and considering the following criteria:

  1. Maximum 10% of the existing students of any discipline/branch shall be considered to change the discipline/branch purely on performance merit basis.
  2. Students who have completed all the examinations of the first two semesters in the first attempt and have minimum CGPA as under, shall be considered for such branch sliding:

a) General Category/EWS/OBC/EBC – 8.0 and above

b) SC/ST Category -  7.0 and above

Training & Placement

_Overview

_BIT Mesra, Ranchi-A Deemed to be University established in the year 1955 have come up with an sprawling campus at Patna in the year 2005 under MoU with Govt. of Bihar. After its establishment, BIT Patna spread over an area of 25 acres has developed as one of the best Technical institutes in the state of Bihar. Currently, BIT Patna offers Engineering(Btech- CSE/AIML/ECE/EEE/Mech/Civil) and Management Courses(MBA/BBA). Our alumni (campus recruits) have been performing well in companies like Google, Amazon, ServiceNow, Paypal, Cashfree, Genpact, Tata Group, Adani, Jindal, Infosy, Wipro, Cognizant, etc. to name a few. Major recruiters ranging from Analytics and Consulting, Automotive Industry, Core Manufacturing, Fintech & Banking Services, Edutech, Electronics & Semiconductors, IT Product/ Services & Technology, Logistics and Supply Chain, Merchant Navy, Real Estate/ Infrastructure, Retail & E-commerce, Telecom sectors, etc. recruited the students from BIT Patna. The placement data can be seen at _www.bitmesra.ac.in

_Placement 2023-24: Total number of 131 recruiters conducted campus drive for BIT Patna students in 2023-24 that has generated 86 numbers of Full Time Employments and 40 numbers of Internship offers across different sectors of the industry. A total of 52 companies conducted the campus process at BIT Patna for the first time this year and several meritorious students have received multiple offers. 42 students have received an annual salary of more than INR 6 Lacs. The highest CTC offered is INR 34.44 Lacs, lowest CTC offered was INR 3 Lacs and the average CTC is 6.63 Lacs per annum for the year 2023-24.

_Placement 2024-25: Total number of 127 recruiters (includes both at Mesra & Patna campus) conducted campus drive for BIT Patna students in 2024-25 that has generated 45 numbers of Full Time Employments and 12 numbers of Internship offers across different sectors of the industry. 32 students have received an annual salary of more than INR 6 Lacs. The highest CTC offered is INR 34.44 Lacs, lowest CTC offered was INR 3.3 Lacs and the average CTC is 7.08 Lacs per annum for the year 2024-25.

Recreational/Extra Curricular activities

The Institute has comprehensive facilities for outdoor sports and games. BIT hosts several extracurricular activities, necessary for overall healthy development of young mind. It has a big play ground, basket ball courts, volleyball courts, lawn tennis courts, badminton courts, well-equipped gymnasiums with modern multi-gym facilities are available. In addition, we have a well-trained coach and guide to support and encourage sports activities for the students.

There are large number of Student Societies and Clubs, which supports students to nurture their hobbies and other creative talents for relaxation and entertainment. These Societies and Clubs include, PRATIBIMB Dramatics & Cultural Society, Megapixel Photographic Club, Students Chapter of IEEE, Literally Club, DIVYA Society for Soft Skills, Robotics Society, SAE Chapter- Automobile, ASME Student Society, IGNITE Technological Society etc.

 BIT PATNA provides the students with many opportunities to demonstrate their talent on a platform much wider than their immediate peer circle in the form of sports events like the INTER COLLEGE SPORTS FEST, ANNUAL ATHLETIC MEET and various inter/intra state sports meet. Student’s of BIT Patna every year organises the tech fest TECHNIKA to showcase their talent and come up with their unique and innovative ideas. Several traditional festivals like Saraswati Puja and Viswakarma Puja are also celebrated with great devotion on campus.

For the up-liftment and empowerment of the underprivileged children groups, National Social Services (NSS) group of the institute organizes various activities like National level RD & Pre RD Camps, Tree Plantation Ceremony, Blood Donation Camp, and Yoga Day, Cancer Day, Youth Parliament etc.

Various Committees and Cells like Women Cell (ICC), Schedule Cast/Schedule Tribes, Anti ragging Committee and Squads have been constituted as per the norms of regulatory bodies.
