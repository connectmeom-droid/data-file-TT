About the Institute

Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya (GSV), a Central University under the Ministry of Railways, Govt. of India, is only University in the Transportation and Logistics sectors in India. 

The University offers unique programs in technology and management with an applied focus on transportation and logistics. The University imparts education through experiential learning to create highly skilled professionals. 

Being an Industry-driven and Innovation-led University with a mandate to significantly impact national development, GSV has established collaborations with prestigious national and international academic institutions and leading industries.

Academic Structure

GSV offers highly impactful B.Tech. and MBA programs based on a demand-driven curriculum to meet the emerging requirements of industry with an interdisciplinary pedagogical approach. The programs are facilitated by advanced technologies, high-quality faculty with global exposure and leveraging state of the art infrastructure of Centralized Training Institutes (CTIs) of Indian Railways. 

The academic structure of GSV consists of 2 Schools and 2 Centers namely:

School of Technology 
School of Management
Center for Executive and Continuing Education 
Center for Policy 

The salient features of academics at GSV are:

  1. Multi-disciplinary techno-management curriculum
  2. Project based learning 
  3. 21st century skills- communication, critical thinking and collaboration
  4. Highly experienced in-house and visiting faculty with international exposure
  5. Global - academic and industry partnerships

Rules For Change Branch

There is a provision for branch change after the first year subject to the merit (the first year CGPA) and the following conditions: 

  1. Seat availability in the intended branch
  2. Passed all the first year courses in first attempt
  3. No Disciplinary Action
  4. The students having 9.0 and above CGPA are allowed branch change upto 120% of the intake of the desired branch.
  5. The strength of the branch from which a student intend to move to another branch should not get reduced to less than 80% of its (admitted) strength.

Financial Assistance

GSV offers financial aid (scholarship) for up to 20% (10% under each income slab) of the admitted eligible students based on merit and family income for each of the following family income slabs:

Financial Aid (Annual) is available in two slabs:

 (a)      Slab-I :  Gross Family Annual Income below 1.0 Lac

            Financial Aid:  100% of Tuition Fee and 50% of Hostel Fee

           

 (b)      Slab-II:  Gross Family Annual Income between Rs. 1.0 Lac and 3.0 Lacs

            Financial Aid:  50% of Tuition Fee and 25% of Hostel Fee

The Financial Aid will be determined based on merit -cum-means of the applicant i.e. Admission merit in the First Year & Academic performance in the subsequent years and the annual family income. 20% of the Financial Aids are reserved for female students

Training & Placement

Gati Shakti Vishwavidyalaya (GSV) provides placement support to the graduating students. University has set-up a Career Development Cell (CDC) which organizes training programs and campus placements.  The CDC is manned by a full time Placement Officer and a faculty as Chairman. The CDC makes every effort to facilitate placements of the graduating students by inviting recruiters for campus placements. There has been good placements of the batches graduated so far. The recruiters, at GSV, include public and private companies viz. Siemens, Alstom, L&T, AARVEE Associates, Nippon Koei, Jindal Stainless Steel, TCE, DFCCIL, RITES, HITACHI Vantara, Tata Projects, Mahindra Logistics, Transworld, PGP Glass, etc.
