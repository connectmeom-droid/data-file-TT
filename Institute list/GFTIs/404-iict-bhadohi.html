About the Institute

The Indian Institute of Carpet Technology popularly known as IICT, the only of its kind in Asia, has been set up by Ministry of Textiles, Govt. of India to provide much needed support to Textile, Carpet and allied industries. The Institute is under the administrative control of the O/o Development Commissioner (Handicrafts), and affiliated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. The B. Tech. (Carpet & Textile Technology) course runs by the institute is approved by A.I.C.T.E., New Delhi and accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The main thrust areas of the institute are A) Human Resource Development B) Design Creation and Development, C) Research & Development and D) Technical Support Services to the industry. The institute Laboratory is also accredited by National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL), Ministry  of Science & Technology, Govt. of India. For details please visit the website: http://iict.ac.in/

Facilities

The campus is spread over 25 acres of land which provides peaceful atmosphere for studies and research. Beside essential infrastructure for technical education, the campus has facilities like separate Hostels for Boys & Girls, residential accommodation for employees, open Air Theater for performing cultural activities, open space for Games, 24 hour electricity supply backed by generator and water supply system.

Student Life at Institute

Green Campus with various types of trees, plants, flower gardens. Separate Hostels for Boys and Girls. Open Air Theater (OAT) for cultural activities. Sports activities in various greenery field. Conference Hall  for lecture/seminar. Annual day function by students along with publication of annual magazine. The details of hostel facilities at IICT can be found in the following link : http://iict.ac.in/Hostel_Facilities_2023.pdf

Location and Accessibility

IICT is situated in Bhadohi which is famous all over the world for handmade carpets. The campus of IICT is 4 Kms from Bhadohi Railway Station (BOY) and situated on Main Road known as Chauri Road on the outskirts of Bhadohi city. It is also well-connected through rail and road from major places such as holy city Varanasi (45 Kms), Prayagraj (95 Kms) and Mirzapur (30 Kms).
