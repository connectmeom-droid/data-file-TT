About the Institute

_The Central University of Jammu (CUJ), established in 2011 under the Central Universities Act,2009, is a premier institution of higher education located in the Samba district of Jammu, Jammu & Kashmir. Spanning 610 acres in the scenic Rahya-Suchani village of Bagla region, the university offers a serene environment conducive to learning and research. CUJ is committed to academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility, providing a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as Engineering, Business Studies, Life Sciences, Basic and Applied Sciences, National Security Studies, Humanities & Social Sciences, Mass Communication and Media Studies, Educational Studies and Languages.

_Equipped with modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, and vibrant student facilities, CUJ emphasizes interdisciplinary research, inclusivity, and community outreach. The university regularly organizes workshops, seminars, and cultural events, fostering a dynamic campus life. With a vision to address regional and global challenges, CUJ has established itself as a center for quality education and holistic development, nurturing talent to contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.

_University has started 04 B.Tech. programmes under two departments i) Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), and ii) Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). Our departments are at the cutting edge of technological advancement, committed to shaping the innovators and leaders of tomorrow.

_In the CSE department, students delve into areas such as cyber security, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, and IoT. Our curriculum balances theoretical foundations with practical applications, supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and real-world projects. Graduates leave prepared for dynamic careers in software development, systems architecture, and beyond.

_The ECE department offers a robust education in the design and application of electronic devices and communication systems. Our students engage with contemporary topics like Avionics, VLSI design, embedded systems, and wireless communication, ensuring they are adept in both established and emerging technologies. This hands-on approach prepares our alumni for impactful roles in telecommunications, aerospace, and various high-tech industries.

_University has two Centre of Excellence i) ISRO sponsored Satish Dhawan Centre for Space Science (SDCSS), and ii) DRDO Industry Academia- Kalam Centre of Excellence (DIA-KCoE). The B.Tech. Electronics and Communication Engineering (Avionics) is mentored by IIST Thirruvananthapuram. 

_Our distinguished faculty, recognized leaders in their fields, bring a wealth of research expertise and industry experience to the classroom. We also provide numerous opportunities for interdisciplinary collaboration, internships, and global exchange programs.

_We invite you to explore our prospectus and discover the diverse opportunities that await. Join us in pushing technological boundaries and making a significant impact on the world.

Training & Placement

The Training and Placement Cell at the Central University of Jammu is instrumental in preparing students for successful careers. It offers a comprehensive suite of training programs designed to enhance both soft and technical skills. Regular workshops focus on communication, personality development, and interpersonal skills, while specialized technical training sessions keep students abreast of the latest industry-relevant technologies.

The cell fosters strong industry connections, facilitating on-campus recruitment drives where leading companies from various sectors seek out talented graduates. These drives, along with strategic internships, provide students with invaluable real-world experience and direct pathways to employment. The cell also organizes mock interviews and group discussions, equipping students with the confidence and skills needed to excel in actual job interviews.

Career counseling services help students navigate their career choices, while assistance with resume building and job applications ensures they present their qualifications effectively. The cell leverages a robust alumni network, creating opportunities for mentorship and professional networking.

With a commendable placement record, the Training and Placement Cell at the Central University of Jammu bridges the gap between academic learning and professional requirements. It ensures that students are not only academically proficient but also well-prepared to meet the demands of the modern workforce, paving the way for successful and fulfilling careers.
