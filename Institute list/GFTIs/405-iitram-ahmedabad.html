About the Institute

The Institute of Infrastructure, Technology, Research and Management (IITRAM) was established by the Government of Gujarat as an autonomous university vide the Institute of Infrastructure, Technology, Research and Management Act, 2012 (Gujarat Act No. 5 of 2013). It has been mandated to bring significant advancements in engineering education, particularly in technical and managerial knowledge related to infrastructure. The Institute aims to serve as a center of excellence for research and teaching in all areas pertaining to infrastructure, with the vision of attaining the status of national importance in this field.

Institute of Infrastructure, Technology, Research And Management offers Bachelor and Master of Technology programs in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. The Institute also offers Ph.D. studies in various fields in Basic Sciences, Engineering, Humanities and Social Sciences. The medium of instruction is English and the examination is also conducted in English.

Vision

Institute of Infrastructure, Technology, Research And Management has been established to contribute to the community of world-leading researchers by promoting excellence within disciplines and inter-disciplinary teams, tackling global challenges, providing cultural enrichment and thereby, benefitting the economy. With a continuously updated curriculum, the University strives to achieve excellence in teaching and research, with a vision to be amongst the best institutions in our country and abroad. One of the aims of the University is to provide a conducive environment that fosters multi-front skill development and overall growth of the students. By enhancing the teaching and assessment methods continuously, and keeping up with the latest technologies, the University aims to achieve excellence in student learning experience. The development of scientific and technical team work, the nourishment of the expertise in scientific innovation and interdisciplinary research are the basic constituents of the University. The University strives to make continuous efforts towards establishing quality and transparent value system for education, research and entrepreneurship

Academic Structure

Institute of Infrastructure, Technology, Research And  Management  (IITRAM), situated in the throbbing city of  Ahmedabad in Maninagar  area, is established as a  University under Gujarat State Legislature Act no. 5 of 2013.

IITRAM is governed by the Board of Governors (BoG). BoG  comprises of Chairman, Director General, three ex-officio Secretary from Government of Gujarat, two academicians,  One VC or ex-VC and three experts from different areas.

The Academic Council is the principal academic body of the  University. It consists of the Director General (Chairman of  the council), three academicians, two directors, two Deans  and Faculties of University school.

The Finance Committee consists of the Chairman, one  member of BoG, Director General, Secretory of Gujarat  Finance Department, ex-officio, and one expert in the field of  finance nominated by BoG.

Registrar is the Secretary of the BoG, the Academic Council  as well as the Finance Committee.

The university has one School of Engineering academic unit with offering Four undergraduate B.Tech program in Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Engineering streams. The Post Graduate program in Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering has been started from academic session 2016-17. The Institute has started Ph. D. programs from the academic session 2016-17.

The Institute also offers Honours / Minor and Micro specialization programs and having state of art laboratory and Center of Excellence. for more details like curriculum etc. please visit https://iitram.ac.in/btech

Rules For Change Branch

The institute may permit a student to change from one branch to another after first two semester strictly on the basis of merit of the applicants. It will be maximum 10% to the subject of availability of seats based on merit after first two semesters. 

Only those students will be eligible for consideration for a change of a branch after the second semester, who have 

·      Completed all the credits prescribed in the first two semesters of their studies in their first attempts 

·      Obtained a CPI not lower than 8.0 at the end of semester 2

Financial Assistance

Information Regarding Scholarships & Awards

The Institute (IITRAM), Ahmedabad, assists its students in applying for various external scholarships and encourages them by offering different types of scholarships. The Institute also supports students in need of financial aid through selective scholarship amounts and fee waivers. Additionally, there are special scholarship schemes for SC, ST, female, OBC, and EWS students provided by various state and central governments.

Some scholarships are offered by private companies, the Government of India, or the concerned state governments based on the student's residence. There are specific procedures for obtaining scholarships, and each application is thoroughly checked and verified before being approved.

As part of the Prime Minister's Digital India program, the Government of India (GoI) has established the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) for students across the country, offering detailed information about various GoI scholarships.

Earn While You Learn:

The Earn While You Learn (EWYL) program aims to provide students with firsthand employment experience. This program helps students gain high-quality job experience, develop leadership and life skills, and take on higher responsibility. Additionally, it provides an opportunity to earn money and meet the Institute's human resource requirements. Students also contribute significantly to the Institute’s continuing education programs and other initiatives.

Teaching Assistantships (Institute-Sponsored):

The Institute offers teaching assistantships (TA) to Ph.D. students, where they assist faculty members, sections, or departments in the smooth conduct of courses. Ph.D. students with strong academic performance are eligible to apply. (Part-time and sponsored candidates, as well as fellowship/scholarship awardees, are not eligible.)

Academic Excellence Awards:

The Institute recognizes the academic achievements of its students by granting several academic excellence awards at the end of each academic year, based on the performance in the previous year. Academic Excellence Awards are given to students with a CPI of 8.5 and above.

Training & Placement

Institute of Infrastructure Technology Research and Management (IITRAM) nurtures a culture of excellence, innovation, and professionalism, which is reflected in the students’ approach to learning and problem solving. They are encouraged to explore real-world challenges, stay attuned to emerging technologies, and develop the adaptability required to excel in complex and ever evolving professional environments.

To channel this potential and ensure that students are equipped not only with academic knowledge but also with the skills necessary to succeed in their careers, the Career Development Centre (CDC) was established at IITRAM. The CDC serves as a strategic interface between the institute and the industry, with the goal of fostering career readiness, bridging the gap between classroom learning and corporate expectations, and providing students with access to opportunities that align with their ambitions and capabilities.

CDC plays a pivotal role in grooming students for the workforce through a structured ecosystem that includes technical training, soft skills development, industry exposure, internships, and placement support. At the same time, it provides a seamless and customized recruitment experience for organizations seeking capable, motivated, and well-prepared graduates.

We invite organizations to collaborate with the CDC at IITRAM and discover a new generation of professional engineers and innovators ready to make a difference.
