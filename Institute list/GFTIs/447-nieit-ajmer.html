About the Institute

Established in 2012, NIELIT Kekri (Ajmer) is a significant campus of the National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (NIELIT), now a Deemed-to-be University. Functioning under the aegis of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, NIELIT Kekri is dedicated to advancing education, research, and skill development in Information, Electronics, and Communication Technology (IECT). The campus is a hub for nurturing talent, empowering individuals with industry-relevant skills, and contributing to the regionâ€™s socio-economic development.

NIELIT Kekri offers a diverse portfolio of programs catering to students, professionals, and government officials. These range from formal academic courses in engineering and diplomas to short-term and advanced certification programs in emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Embedded Systems, IoT, Networking, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering. The center also emphasizes digital and financial literacy, equipping learners with essential skills for the digital era. Its innovative programs, including internships under the PM SHRI scheme for school students and specialized corporate training for government officials, reflect its commitment to holistic learning and inclusivity.

Beyond education, NIELIT Kekri is actively involved in research, development, and consultancy services, particularly in e-governance and electronic systems. These efforts align with the broader goals of MeitY to support India's digital transformation journey and position the country as a global leader in technology and innovation.
