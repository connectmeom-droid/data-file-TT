About the Institute

The Institute was set up initially as a project of the North Eastern Council (NEC) for providing a system of education to create technical manpower at various levels for the development of the North Eastern Region of the country. The North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology (NERIST) is a unique Institute in the country, having unconventional and innovative academic Programmes. It was established in the year 1983 by the Government of India. Its foundation stone was laid by the former President of India Late Gyani Zail Singh on 4th March, 1984. It is autonomous and fully funded by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India since April 1, 1994. It got the status of a DEEMED TO BE UNIVERSITY on 31st May 2005 under section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. It is governed by the Board of Management (BoM) comprising representatives of the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India, the eight beneficiary states of the North East India, AICTE and eminent educationists. It is residential, and is spread over 513 acres of land. All the Under graduates and Post Graduates Engineering Courses are approved by AICTE, New Delhi. All the under graduate programs  were accredited by NBA upto 30.06.2027 and the Under Graduate program of Electrical Engineering has provisionally accredited  upto 30.06.2025.  Academic Programmes in the Institute started during August 1986 Agricultural Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Forestry. Our graduates have been placed in reputed organizations at national and international levels. There are 12 (Twelve) degree awarding departments including a Center for Management Studies. The Institute offers bachelor’s degree programmes in 6 (six) Engineering disciplines and one in Forestry. The engineering disciplines are Agricultural Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Besides the bachelor’s degree programmes, several post-graduate degree programmes leading to M.Tech, M.Sc, MBA and Ph.D. are also offered by the Institute.

Academic Structure

Technology stream (i.e., Engineering branches) at NERIST has  two independent modules, viz., Base (Certificate) and Degree modules. The first entry is at the Base Module after Class X through NEE-I. Lateral entry or lateral admission into Degree modules are permitted through JEE , NEE-II and NEE-III respectively. Normal duration of Base module is two years (i.e., four Semesters). Duration of degree module admitted through NEE-II and JEE  is 4 years while admitted through NEE-III is 3 years. The Base levels emphasize more on practical aspects of engineering which go a long way in helping an individual to become an entrepreneur or face day-to-day problems of industry.  The degree curriculum provides instructions in Engineering  and Technology domin and also emphasizes on management and entrepreneurship aspects making an individual more suited to the needs of today’s industries.  Forestry stream, on the other hand, has entry point after Class-XII with Physics, Chemistry and Biology through NEE-II for admission to Degree Module leading to B.Sc degree in Forestry of 4 years’ duration (eight Semesters). There are 12 (Twelve) degree awarding departments including a Center for Management Studies. The Institute offers bachelor’s degree programmes in 6 (six) Engineering disciplines and one in Forestry. The engineering disciplines are Agricultural Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Besides the bachelor’s degree programmes, several post-graduate degree programmes leading to M.Tech, M.Sc, MBA and Ph.D. are also offered by the Institute

Student Life at Institute

NERIST provides a vibrant ambience for the students. Located in the lap of the verdant nature surrounded by mountains and rivers, the NERIST campus is one of the beautifully located campuses in the region.  It provides a unique opportunity and environment for the students to learn the importance of sustainable growth while maintaining harmony with nature.  With a unique pattern of education, NERIST gets its pool of intake primarily from the eight north eastern states of the region and a few students join from the other states of India. This provides a rich comingling of cultures for the students and it breeds a healthy habit of cultural tolerance and expose them to a vibrant cultural mix which helps in widening their outlook and understanding.  While the institute follows a strict academic calendar, the rigor of academics, however, doesn’t dampen the spirit of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities as the students get ample platforms to hone and display their latent talent. The various Departmental forums and creative clubs help them to dabble in their hobbies round the year and the sporting and cultural events help them grow into individuals who could shine and face the challenges of life with élan.

Financial Assistance

The students can apply for various scholarships schemes available to them as per eligibility through National Scholarship Portal (NSP).The SC/ST students of North Eastern states are generally provided with stipends by the concerned state and Central governments at varying rates. The North Eastern Council (NEC) also provides scholarship to the students of the region for their study at Degree and higher levels. The continuance of stipend is subject to the following conditions: (i)  His/her maintenance of discipline in the Institute is consistently good and he/she is regular in the classes. Unauthorized absence from the Institute/Hostel is treated as a lapse of discipline. (ii) Satisfactory progress in his/her studies.

Industry And Alumni Relations

The NERIST graduates dot the globe today and the result is a strong global alumni base showcasing their expertise ranging from engineering to management, and research to technopreneurial ventures. Some of the prominent organizations where the NERIST alumni are currently placed include Google, Microsoft, NASA, Adobe, etc. This apart, the NERIST alumni plays an active role in mentoring the current students, particularly in the areas of technological innovation and monetizing their ventures.  The NERIST alumni plays a pivotal role in placement activities of the institute as well. Location and Accessibility

The Institute is located in a picturesque valley at the foot-hills of the Eastern Himalayas, and is situated at Nirjuli (Itanagar) in Papum Pare district of Arunachal Pradesh on National Highway 415 (NH415). It is about 350 kms from Guwahati (Assam) and 10 kms from Banderdewa, the Entry Gate to the Capital Complex, Itanagar (Arunachal Pradesh). From the Institute, the capital town, Itanagar is about 18 kms. onward.  Arunachal Pradesh being the land of exotic orchids and lush green hills, it provides an ideal setting for a seat of technical institution in the backdrop of quiet and pristine surroundings. The nearest airports are located at Hollongi, Itanagar approx 35 km , Tezpur(180 km) and Lilabari /North Lakhimpur(40 km) , Guwahati(approx. 350 km) respectively. The Institute can be accessed by rail (Nearest Railway Station is Naharlagun (approx. 07 km from the Institute) and road, Super Delux buses/Train facility daily to and from Guwahati. Taxis can also be hired at Guwahati to reach the Institute.
