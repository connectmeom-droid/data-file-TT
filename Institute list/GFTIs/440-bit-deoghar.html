About the Institute

The Deoghar Campus of Birla Institute of Technology was established in 2007 as a joint venture of Government of Jharkhand and Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. The institute is situated in  Deoghar at a distance of 1.5-2.0 Km from Jasidih station. Deogharis  an Important pilgrimage city of Jharkhand and is known for one of the twelve Jyotirlingas - Baijnath Dham in the heart of the city where pilgrims from all over the world visit the city throughout the year to worship Lord Shiva. Besides this Ram Krishna Mission, Swami Anukul Chand Ashram, Rikhia Ashram and many other important places of worship justify its name ‘’Deoghar’’. The city can boast of first AIIMS in the State of Jharkhand and is well connected by Road, Rail and Air to all parts of the country .

 

A highly knowledgeable and experienced faculty can help students discover their hidden talents, provide doors for them to realize their full potential and help them acquire a balanced mix of technical, managerial and interpersonal skills. This fosters their development as future business personnel, entrepreneurs and a good human being. To accomplish this Institute has Highly Qualified faculties on its roll. Besides this, Experts from BIT main Campus keep visiting the Campus regularly to conduct special workshops and seminars for students and faculties.

 

The Institute can accommodate more than 1000 students in its two boy's hostels and one girl's hostel. It has an advanced library acquiring a good number of books and e-resources including e-books and e-journals, laboratories with modern equipment, and spectacular sports facilities for both students and employees.

 

The Institute offers four- year B.Tech. course in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) and Mechanical Engineering (ME).

Rules For Change Branch

Eligibility Criteria for Branch Change:

i. Students admitted in the B. Tech  program shall only be eligible to apply for change of branch after the completion of first year.

ii. Students must have completed all the prescribed credits of the first year (1st and 2nd semesters) in the first attempt and must have an average SGPA (1st and 2nd semesters) as:

(a) 8.00 or above for General/EWS/OBC/EBC

(b) 7.00 or above for SC/ST

iii. Students should not have received any fail grade (F/F*/UFM) in any course to be eligible for branch change.

iv. Students should not have faced any disciplinary action or indulged in an act of indiscipline.

V. Students admitted under Gol/NRI/PIO/OCI/SFFS categories shall not be eligible for branch change.

Note:

a. The seats shall be filled based on category in which the student had taken admission.

b. Branch change shall be done strictly in order of merit, availability of category wise vacancies and preferences opted by the students.

c. In case of a tie between two or more students, branch change shall be done based on CRL of JEE (Main).

d. Sliding/transfer of students may be allowed till the strength does not fall below 65% of the sanctioned intake of the concerned department.

e. Once a branch has been allocated to a student, no request for further change/revert of the allocated branch shall be entertained under any circumstances.
