About the Institute

Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University (CSVTU) is a State Government Technical University located in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, India. 

Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University (CSVTU) was established by an act (No. 25 of 2004) of legislature passed by the Chhattisgarh State Govt. Assembly vide notification no. 639/21-A/Praroopan/04 dt 21st January 2005 to incorporate a University and Technology for the purpose of ensuring systematic, efficient and quality education in engineering and technological subjects including Architecture and Pharmacy at Research, Post graduate, Degree and Diploma level.

The University since its inception in the year 2005 is striving hard to emerge as one of the nations prominent Universities to fulfill its commitment to the service of state and nation. It was inaugurated on 30th April 2005 by the Hon’ble Prime-Minister of India Dr. Manmohan Singh. The University without waiting for full fledged infrastructure development started identifying the frontier area of research and development and outreach programmes for the benefit of the society. With this approach several academic programmes, seminars, workshops and conferences have been conducted by the University during past 5 years.

Hareli Plantation Programme - 2022

The Chhattisgarh state celebrates its cultivation greenery, gained through several months of perseverance, as Hareli Festival. The name of this festival comes from the Hindi word "Haryali" meaning greenery. This abounds either in the month of July or August (Sawan Amavasya– a holy month for farmers) as the annual monsoon season is in its full swing. It is an agricultural festival celebrated by the many rural farming communities in the state by worshipping their agricultural tools and equipments. Emphasizing its importance, the state government has declared a regional public holiday on Hareli as an initiative to celebrate state’s popular indigenous festival on the grand scale

Raising the cultural awareness of local traditions and importance of natural asset among the youth and the society, the CSVTU Family organized a One Day Hareli Plantation Programme in the university campus on 27th July 2022. The key objective was to indulge all the students and staff members to promote the tree plantation to save the endangered environment and species. The chief dignitaries Dr. Prof. P. K. Ghosh, Director, CSVTU Bhilai, Dr. K.K. Verma, Registrar, CSVTU Bhilai and Mr. Vinay Kumar Karkashe, P.W.D. Department, CSVTU Bhilai, bestowed their significance presence. Additionally, all the UTD faculties and staff members, research scholars along with the students of B.Tech. M. Tech and Diploma courses actively participated in the programme.

Cultural event on occasion of 76_th Independence Day celebration

76_th Independence Day (Azaddi ka Amrit Mahatsova) was celebrated at Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekananda Technical University, Bhilai with great patriotic fervor and zeal. The Hon’ble Vice-Chancellor Dr. M.K. Verma, accompanied by Registrar, Director, Staff Members and students, Unfurled the tri-colour flag, followed by the National Anthem. Later, a cultural program was also held in the University Teaching Department (UTD). This cultural program was organized by the Sports and Cultural club of UTD. The program began with “Rajkiya Geet” performed by the UG students. Students made speeches, presented patriotic songs, group dance and recited poems. After the cultural program Hon’ble V.C. Chancellor addresses the gathering. In his discourse he shares the glory of our mother land and advises the students how they can be a very integral part and contribute their talent in building India, a strong nation.
