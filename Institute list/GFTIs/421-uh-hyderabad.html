About the Institute

The University of Hyderabad (UoH) is a central university with the prestigious Institution of Eminence status. It was established by an Act of Parliament in 1974.

It is one of the major institutions of higher education in India is largely devoted to postgraduate studies and is widely known for its excellence in research and for its distinguished faculty. The large green campus located near Gachibowli, which is the IT hub of Hyderabad, has immense bio-diversity, not to speak of the lakes and rock formations
which give the campus a natural beauty.

The University has built up a global reputation for providing high quality academic training, and has been recognized through a number of awards. With 12 schools, 43 departments and 13 centres, UoH has a vibrant learning environment. Among these schools the School of Computer & Information Sciences and the School of Engineering Sciences & Technology offer the I.M.Tech Programs, Computer Science & Engineering and Materials Engineering,
respectively. More details are given below.

Faculty

The School of Computer and Information Sciences (SCIS)

(see website _https://scis.uohyd.ac.in/) offers excellence in all the major aspects of higher learning in teaching, research, student development and curriculum planning in all subjects related to Computer Science and AI. The strengths of the School are its quality faculty, innovative and flexible curricula and with an emphasis on research. The practices in the school are very open for young students and with transparent policies that foster a healthy student-faculty interaction. The School provides an environment conducive for placement and student progression to higher education depending on the inclination of the learners.


Opportunities for those joining Integrated Five Year  M.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) students

The admissions to I.MTech (CSE) are very competitive with the best in JOSA opting for it. These students who join the program are trained by highly qualified faculty in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. These students also acquire a sound knowledge of Mathematics, Sciences and other topics in Engineering. The University of Hyderabad boasts of a learning environment to give a well rounded education in many disciplines through the choices available to them in Humanities, Languages and other disciplines as per NEP. As the program is recognized by AICTE, the students who are successful in GATE are eligible for GATE Fellowships in the 5th year. Students also go for internships in many companies through the placement unit of the University. I.MTech (CSE) students have also successfully enrolled in doctoral programs abroad.


Historical Notes

SCIS, created in 2012, is highly focused on Computer Science, AI and Applications with its courses on I MTech (CSE)(through JOSA after +2 level of School Education), M. Tech in AI and CS admission through GATE, MCA (admission through NIMCET common Test) and PhD (CS) (admission through UGC-NET-JRF). The School has more than 500 students on its rolls. SCIS is well known throughout India and abroad for the quality of its research with four faculty in the Stanford list of top-2% researchers world-wide. Currently  faculty strength is about 27 and they perform quality research in cutting edge areas such as Deep Learning, AI, Internet of Things, Parallel and Cloud Computing, Cryptography, etc. The curriculum is flexible, with a mix of core subjects and state-of-the-art electives preparing the student for both employment and research.


Historically SCIS has been involved in computer science education through its MCA, M.Tech and Ph.D. programmes as a part of the School of Mathematics/Computer and Information Sciences since 1983. The Department of Computer and Information Sciences was created in 1993 and later became a fully independent school in 2012. It has one of the first and the oldest running AI programmes in the country with more than 800 students graduated thus far. SCIS also started the highly successful 5-year Integrated M. Tech (CSE) programme in 2014 and since then SCIS has been a destination of choice for those clearing their Plus two education as it offers an excellent place for students to train in Computer Science education with its quality faculty, 24x7 research labs, advanced high performance computing facilities and attractive placements. The students regularly win awards at national hackathons, participate and win prizes in many inter-collegiate events and publish papers in reputed conferences and journals. Many of our alumni are in highly-ranked colleges and universities both in India and abroad, are in senior-level positions in extremely well-known companies and some are successful entrepreneurs.



The School of Engineering Sciences and Technology (SEST) was established in the academic year 2008-09 with an objective to impart high quality engineering education and pursue high quality research in emerging multi-disciplinary areas encompassing science, engineering and technology.
SEST Courses:

At present, SEST offers Ph.D. programmes in Materials Engineering as well as in Nanoscience and Technology. Integrated B.Tech+M.Tech (I.M.Tech) programme in Materials Engineering.

SEST Facilities:

SEST provides an ideal environment to pursue cross-disciplinary research in engineering sciences and technology by taking advantage of the well-established facilities and expertise available within the school and in the University campus. Cutting-edge high-end research facilities for Materials Engineering and Nano
Science and Technology are available, including various synthesis, characterization and computational facilities. SEST also collaborates with premier research institutions located in and around Hyderabad (namely DMRL, IICT, ARCI, NFC, NFTDC and RCI), most of which are also formally recognized as schoolâ€™s external
research centers. SEST also does international collaborative research activities and exchange programs with several countries.

SEST Faculty and research:

All faculty members have Ph.D from prestigious national and international institutes with a strong research and teaching expertise in various advanced research domains including, structural and functional materials, energy, damage monitoring, additive manufacturing etc., The school, has been able to attract research grants of more than Rs. 20 crores from various external funding agencies. Faculty members of the school have published more than 600 publications and 30 patents. For more information check out the website of SEST, UoH.
