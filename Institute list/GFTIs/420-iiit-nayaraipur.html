About the Institute

Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee IIIT-Naya Raipur, established by the International Institute of Information Technology University Act, 2013 of the Government of Chhattisgarh, is a joint venture of Chhattisgarh State Government and National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC). The institute is committed to pursue excellence in higher education, research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines. It firmly believes in bestowing knowledge dissemination and imparting entrepreneurial skills to the students to enable them to address real-world problems. To empower this vision, IIIT-NR provides state-of-the-art research and education facilities and nurtures a campus culture that fosters high energy and enthusiasm in every individual, clubbed with the highest standard of professionalism. For more information, please visit www.iiitnr.ac.in.

Facilities

The 50 acres lush green campus of IIIT-NR is enabled with Wi-Fi connectivity, excellent amenities for sports and other recreational activities. The modern architecture of the buildings and the serene environment of the campus fosters an ambience for the students for creativity and innovation. The institute has two state-of-art virtual classrooms. The classrooms and laboratories are equipped with state-of-the-art technologies for teaching and learning.

Parents and guardians of students are allowed to stay in IIIT-NR guest house at nominal charges subject to availability of rooms and permission from concerned authorities. Rooms are air-conditioned, furnished and provided with attached bathrooms. Guests can avail on-campus dining facilities at nominal charges.

Student Life at Institute

Hostel Facilities: Presently, IIIT-NR has a boys hostel and a girls hostel. Two additional hostels are under construction. Students can access campus network and computing facilities from their hostel rooms through high-speed WiFi. All students are required to stay in the hostel.

Canteen Facilities: The institute has separate dining facilities for boys and girls in their respective hostels. Additionally, it has a centralized canteen that serves snacks, non-veg food, general fast food, juice, and milk products.  Institute mess serves both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food. There is a cafeteria at multi purpose hall. The mess is managed by a committee comprising of students, staff, and faculty.

Sports Facilities: Every hostel has separate sports facility for indoor games including badminton. For outdoor games, two volleyball courts and one football cum cricket ground is available. A basketball court cum four badminton courts multi purpose indoor sports complex of international standards is available. Two table-tennis tables are also available in the multi-purpose hall.

Gym: Every hostel has an open air Gym facility with modern equipment. A centralized indoor Gym facility is also available.

Open Air Theatre: Institute has two open air theatres (Amphi theatres) for its cultural activities.

Auditorium: The institute has an auditorium with 1000 seats capacity for organizing large technical and cultural events.

Health: Health insurance in addition to ambulance facility is available for all students.

Financial Assistance


  1. Financial aid to needy students is under active consideration by the Govt of Chhattisgarh. Any student entitled to financial aid approved by the Govt. will get back to a refund from already paid fees.
  2. Institute will provide a financial Assistantship of up to Rs 5 Lakhs is provided to carry out a summer internship/Major Project (8th Semester) abroad for selected students (as per the institute guidelines).
  3. Students are encouraged to undertake research work as part of their minor and major projects. The institute will provide financial assistance up to ₹10,000 per student, a maximum of three times within India, and up to ₹100,000 once for travel abroad during their B.Tech program to present their research work at reputed National/International conferences.

Location and Accessibility

The fifty-acre, fully residential campus of IIIT-NR is located in the born smart city of Naya Raipur, the state capital of Chhattisgarh. It is approximately 23 Kms. from the city of Raipur and well connected by air, rail and road from various parts of India. AC and Non-AC BRTC bus service are available from Raipur railway station to the institute.
