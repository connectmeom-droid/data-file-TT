About the Institute

The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme “RASHTRIYA UCHCHATAR SHIKSHA ABHIYAN (RUSA)” has approved for the Upgradation of the erstwhile Pondicherry Engineering College to a Technological University and to incorporate it as an affiliating, teaching and research University at Puducherry to facilitate and promote studies, research, technology incubation, product innovation and extension work in Technology, Management and Science and also to achieve excellence in higher and technical education and other matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.

Puducherry Technological University came into effect from 5th September 2020 officially and started admission under the banner of PTU from the academic year 2020-21.  

A Brief information about Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC):

Pondicherry Engineering College was established in 1984 by MHRD, Government of India under the Seventh Five Year Plan to meet the requirement of an engineering institution in the Union Territory of Puducherry.  Ever since its inception, the University has maintained a steady academic and research growth both vertically and horizontally with an aim to offer quality technical education and cater to the diverse requirements of the industries besides the expectation of the prevailing societal system. The University currently offers 9 UG and fourteen PG programmes in core engineering disciplines besides MCA, M.Sc and PhD programmes in all engineering disciplines and basic sciences. As the first University (erstwhile engineering college) in the Union Territory of Puducherry, it preserves its uniqueness among all professional institutions which were started later. The University enjoys significant autonomy for administration. The institution was initially affiliated to Pondicherry University and has been granted ‘permanent affiliation’ as the first and significant step towards attainment of academic autonomy and obtained Autonomous status from 2014-15 onwards.  

PTU is ranked in the band of (201 – 300) in the Engineering Category by NIRF India Rankings 2024. 

The major strengths of the institution are its location, well-connected by land and air routes to major cities in India, highly qualified faculty with 98% doctorate, diverse student population from across the country and from foreign countries and latest infrastructure facilities with state-of-the-art laboratories in the emerging fields of Engineering / Technology. PTU (erstwhile PEC) alumni are spread far and wide across the globe holding eminent positions in top global Corporates, Universities and Civil Services. The University, preserving its brand name, attracts the meritorious students of UT of Puducherry in addition to students from neighboring states. The placement of students through campus is progressive since inception which includes top recruiters viz., HP, TCS, ZOHO, HCL, QualComm, Mbit Wireless, Accenture, NOKIA, INFOSYS, RELIANCE, CTS, L&T INFOTECH. PTU / PEC is recognized as a feeder institution by many of the Universities in USA, UK and Australia as well.

 In addition to that, Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) PEC Foundation enables the budding entrepreneurs to showcase and test their abilities to run a start up business in the emerging area of engineering / technology.

NPTEL - PTU Ranked as 17th and awarded with AA Rating  at IIT Chennai (2022). PTU competes with 6500+ Local Chapters nationwide and rated AA with 111 Gold Medals and 164 Silver medals out of attended 1027 students (In top 100) during the period Jan-April 2024. 

Rated AA with 402 Gold Medals and 214 Silver medals out of attended 923 students (in top 100) during the period July-December, 2024.

PTU has been rated ‘AAAA’ certification and recognized as the one of the best Engineering institutes in the year 2023 by CAREERS 360. Recently PTU received the prestigious “IEEE India Council award”.

Considering all these aspects, the strategic plan for institutional development has been prepared with the following specific vision: “To evolve into a university of global eminence through transformative learning and research in frontier areas of engineering and technology towards developing holistic technology leaders, innovators and entrepreneurs”.

Academic Structure

All the B.Tech. courses are designed to be delivered in 8 semesters (4 years). Students can enroll for minimum credits as stipulated by the PTU B.Tech. curriculum every semester and earn credits based on the individual's performance evaluated through internal and external examinations, both for theory and practical courses. Students will be doing project work at the 8th semester.

More details on the curriculum, regulations and syllabi can be seen at the Academic Portal.

Rules For Change Branch

The branches allotted to the students at the end of all the rounds of JoSAA and special allocation rounds of CSAB are changeable only at the end of the second semester. The Dean(Academics) will issue a circular to all the students inviting applications for branch change. The branch change will be allotted based on the merit (CGPA) secured by the students during the first two semesters and the availability of vacant seats at the time of counseling for branch change.

PTU admits students under two categories, viz. (i) CENTAC (Centralised Admission Committee, Govt. of Puducherry) for the native students of U.T. of Puducherry (fee payable is Rs. 32,101 approximately) and (ii) JoSAA/CSAB for the Other States quota and All India category (Self-Support seats only - fee payable is Rs.1,62,401 / Rs.87,401). 

Accordingly, the vacant seats, if any under self-support schemes only will be allotted by the Dean(Students) for change of branches subject to the availability and other merit positions. That is, even if vacancies exist at the beginning of second year under CENTAC admitted category, the students admitted through JoSAA/CSAB are not eligible for branch change. The fee applicable for self-support will be applicable for the entire course duration.

If a candidate has registered himself/herself through the CENTAC portal for self-support scheme, then he/she may get a chance to migrate to a branch of their choice at the time of mop up round conducted by CENTAC for the unfilled seats of JoSAA/CSAB. If such a candidate is a native of U.T. of Puducherry, then he/she shall even switch over from Self-Support course to the CENTAC vacancy. This switch over is not admissible for Other State Candidates.

The students having doubts, if any may contact the Admissions Coordinator by sending a mail to admissions@ptuniv.edu.in

Facilities

All the academic departments are equipped with state of the art computing facilities in their respective laboratories.
The campus has a good internet connectivity supporting 1 GBPS link from National Knowledge Network (NKN). Hostels are provided with Wi-Fi access points.
Central Library with plenty of text books and other reading materials, digital library facility is located in a closer proximity with academic buildings.
Canteen facility, Sports (headed by a Director of Physical Education), Attendance Tracking system, academic management system are some important facilities available in the university.

Student Life at Institute

The 180 acre spread green campus of the university is filled with a variety of trees and plants creating a pleasant mood for the students to concentrate on their studies and get relaxed during the off-class timings. A central library located in a closer proximity to the academic departments has a plenty of collection of text books, magazines, journals and news papers. Students can access to digital library services using the campus wide internet facility extended by the National Knowledge Network (NKN).

Two gents hostel and a ladies hostel with good ambience and cuisine caters to the needs of the students residing in the campus. The hostel wardens and staff take extreme care of the resident students at all times. Extension of hostel building works is under proposal. The campus has a resident medical officer with a staff nurse to take care of the first aid and medical assistance to the students and staff members.

Charted buses ply at regular intervals of time to drop and pick up the students between the hostels and the academic departments. Charted buses also ply to drop and pickup the students residing in the city.

A central auditorium accommodating about 800 students caters to the need of university wide events such as university annual day, graduation day, alumni meet, career awareness programmes and cultural programmes such as Rejouir.

A lot of clubs available in the university help the students spend their valuable time in exhibiting their talents.

Existence of Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) PECF inside campus  with a lot of incubators is an unique facility of this campus.

The Training and Placement division nourishes the students right from their second year of graduation to get themselves prepared for grabbing placement opportunities at the beginning of their fourth year of graduation.

Financial Assistance

The Government of Puducherry waives tuition fees for all the students admitted to engineering programmes through Centralised Admission Committee (CENTAC) under the scheme PERUNTHALAIVAR KAMARAJAR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE SCHEME (PKFA). Apart from this there are other financial assistance schemes.

The financial assistance/scholarships granted by the state and central governments can be availed by the eligible students using the official portal https://scholarships.gov.in/ 

Training & Placement

The Training and Placement (TNP) Office is the nodal point of contact for companies to interact with Puducherry Technological University (PTU) Erstwhile Pondicherry Engineering College (PEC). The TNP office maintains a close connection with various companies, with nearly 92 companies visiting the campus every year for campus interviews. To give students exposure to the application of technology in the industry and hands-on work experience, the institute mandates that students complete a minimum of two internships in industries engaged in core industrial activities during their semester holidays. These internships are integrated into the curriculum and are credit-bearing. 

The students of PTU secure placements in various sectors, including Core Engineering, Software/IT, and Finance/Consulting. Major companies like JP Morgan Chase & Co,  Amazon, Microchip, Kaar Technologies, Sanmar, Bounteous, LatentView, Accenture, Tata Consultancy Services, Larsen and Toubro, Mbit Wireless, One Bill, Societe Generale, Cognizant, Mitsogo, PCBL, Avasoft, embedUR, Capgemini, Gabor, Lucas TVS, General Optics Asia Limited, Toshiba, AdminDroid, UST, Mthree Accenture, Tech Mahindra, Eversendai, Wabag, Eleation, Infosys, Emmardesteel, Torrent Gas, Schneider Electric and many other visits our campus for recruitment every year. 

Given the constantly evolving global recruitment landscape, it is crucial for students to acquire a diverse set of skills to maintain their employability. 

  • To this end, we provide training by AICTE-approved training partners every semester for a period of 15 days for 650 students. 

  • Additionally, we offer in-house training by three dedicated trainers in the following areas: Coding, Soft Skills, and Aptitude for 650 students and the training is an integral part of academic curriculum. 

  • This training is conducted for 3 hours per week for each program over a period of 15 weeks per semester from 5th semester onwards.

  • Furthermore, industry readiness programs, featuring speakers from top companies, are organized for final year and third year students to create awareness of the latest technologies.

  • The training modules provide the basic understanding required from Engineering Graduates coming from different disciplines for a smooth transition and preparing for careers in niche and rapidly emerging domains such as Quantum Computing/Simulation, GPU Computing for Acceleration, High Performance Exascale Computing and Networking, Essentials of Cloud Computing Containerization, Virtualization and Orchestration Technology for PLM, Digital Twins and Industry 4.0 Automation for Product Development, FPGA HDL Hardware Programming, CAD 3D Solid Modeling and Meshing, FEM and Computational Fluid Dynamics Modeling and Simulation for Product Development of UAVs/Drones, Project Management for Industrial Engineering and Energy Projects, Advances in AI and XR Technologies, Additive Manufacturing for Industry 4.0, Preparatory Training on BEE Certified Energy Manager cum Auditor, Project Management Certification, Industry 4.0 Certification, Codes, Standards for Professional Engineering Practice, G-Code/CNC Programming for Industry 4.0 Industrial and Intelligent Robotics Automation, Modern Scientific Computing Programming Languages and Mathematical Optimization with Julia, Python, R, Go for AI, ML, DS and QML, Mobile Computing Embedded Real Time Systems IIoT SBCs, Linux/Android OS Platform for Software Development ARM/x86, Workshop on Generative AI, using LaTeX, Multi-Domain Modeling, Simulation and Optimization of Hydraulic Pneumatic Electro Mechanical Energy Process Control for Mechatronic Systems so as to align well with Multidisciplinary Job Roles and become Job Ready

Industry And Alumni Relations

Distinguished Alumni are spread far and wide across the globe occupying coveted positions both in industry and academia. 
Parent Chapter of the Alumni Association is located in the PTU campus, whose activities are coordinated by a faculty member. 
Alumni Association is having its chapters at Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune and Chicago. 
Alumni contribute to the growth of the college and the welfare of the current PTU students. 
Recently, Alumni Association sponsored 11 Reverse – Osmosis water filter systems to departments, contributed Rs. 2.4 lakhs towards construction of Canara Bank ATM in the campus. It has been providing scholarship to 35 meritorious and needy students using the interest generated from its corpus fund titled “PECAA help fund ”. 
The Alumni Association hosts Silver Jubilee Reunion celebrations every year in a grand manner.
NGOs set up by alumni members such as Le Banyan de PEC and SEED Foundation not only extend their support to the poor and meritorious students of PTU by extending scholarships, but also conduct workshops/seminars and career awareness programmes in the campus every year.

The Dean (Alumni and Inter University Collaboration) collaborates with the alumni of PEC and PTU and involve them in curriculum revisions.

Recreational/Extra Curricular activities

The university campus spans for about 180 acres in which there are facilities for students such as Open Air theatre, Football and Tennis courts, Cricket grounds and indoor game facilities.

The university has a centralized and air-conditioned  Auditorium  with seating capacity of 800. The University Day, Convocation, REJOUIR (inter university cultural festival), JIGYASA (inter university technical meet), CURIOSO (Literary Club events) and other year long seminars, symposiums are conducted in this auditorium.
An unit of National Service Scheme (NSS) headed by professors as coordinators organizes camps inside and outside of the PTU campus regularly.

The campus has a well established canteen built with cuisines of multiple options for both north and south Indians with funding support by Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) ( with ) and PONLAIT (An outlet for milk products of both hot and cold)  and a Shopping complex with multiple shops covering all the needs of the students.

Location and Accessibility

The university campus is situated at Pillaichavady which is about 12 km from the Railway station and bus-stand in Pondicherry town. It is about 160 km south of Chennai (Madras) on the shores of Bay of Bengal. The campus is also well connected by town buses from Pondicherry. Buses ply between Chennai (Madras) and Pondicherry frequently on the scenic beauty ECR and the duration of journey is about 2 to 3 hours only. The buses that come from Chennai via East Coast Road (ECR) stop near the campus itself. Commutation by cabs/auto-rickshaws from Pondicherry city. Puducherry Airport to Campus is 20 minutes drive only.
