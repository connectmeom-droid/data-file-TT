About the Institute

For over five decades, BIT Mesra (located 16 kms from Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand) has been engaged in nurturing minds through a rich heritage of academic excellence. A bustling hub of student activities, the beautiful green campus has been a second-home to thousands of students in their journey for their 3-dimensional growth and prosperity.

 Established in 1955 by visionary-industrialist Mr. B.M. Birla, it is today one of the premier engineering destinations in India. The Institute attained the status of a Deemed University under Section 3 of the UGC Act, in 1986. It is a full member of the Association of Indian Universities and the Association of Commonwealth Universities.

Industry And Alumni Relations

Industry and Alumni Relations

_The institute organises alumni reunions for different batches in the campus. We are proud to be one of the very organizations who were blessed to have Diamond Jubilee Alumni Meet (1958 – 1962 Batch) on 29th November 2022. The zeal and enthusiasm with which these alumni meet each other was worth praising. The batch of 1967 – 1972 also celebrated their Golden Jubilee reunion from 15th – 17th October 2022. The batch of 1983 – 1987 celebrated their 35th year of passing out at BIT on 24th December 2022. Infact, alumni reunion is a regular feature at BIT Mesra. Apart from this, BIT has a custom to invite batch passed out 40 years and 25 years ago to celebrate their reunion during the foundation day. In the forthcoming Foundation Day on 15th July 2023, the alumni who passed in 1983 and who passed in 1998 have been invited.

_Alumni portal www.alumni.bitmesra.ac.in which was launched in February 2020, has got a very good response. This platform offers amazing features that enable alumni to stay connected with their colleagues and friends as well as explore professional opportunities. Presently, the institute has a data bank of about 40000+ registered alumni in the portal. The portal has become a valuable tool for engaging alumni across the world.

_The institute has a registered alumni association in the name of BITMAA (Birla Institute of Technology Mesra Alumni Association) to strengthen the relationship and acting as an apex body for alumni engagement and outreach. All the chapters across the globe have their representations in BITMAA. The day-to-day activities of BITMAA is governed by the Steering Committee of Alumni (SCA), which works on the suggestions of the Executive Body of Alumni (EBA). BITMAA has its own bank account. And is 12A and 80G compliant.

_With the support of alumni members in the US, students of undergraduate engineering program (6th Semester) are sent to reputed US universities during the summers under the Immersive Summer Research Experience (ISRE) program. The summer internship is carried out at the Illinois Institute of Technology-Chicago, Carnegie Mellon University-Pittsburgh, Northeastern University-Boston, Columbia University-New York, and Georgia Institute of Technology-Atlanta Till date, Seventy-three students have already been benefitted through the above program. This year, Seventeen students will be visiting above universities to pursue their summer internship. 

_Our alumni members also support us in Faculty Research programs with the objective of exposing our faculty members to contemporary research practices in the US. Dr Shiva Kumar from EEE Department visited North-Eastern University, Boston, during September 2022 to December 2022, with full funding. The nomination process for this year is already initiated.

_Institute celebrated its Foundation Day on the 14th and 15th of July 2022. This is an annual event introduced to reach out to our alumni members spread across the globe in the corporate sector as well as in academics. Distinguished Alumni Awards were conferred to our alumni under six different categories, who have made us proud in India and abroad through their contributions. 

_The alumni members also support the students at the institute by offering mentorship in their domain of expertise. This mentorship is being offered by our esteemed alumni across the globe who are well-settled in academia or industry to train the students and make them industry ready.

_Academic and Research Collaboration with industry and technical institutes in India and abroad is also arranged by the institute.

_To impart and share knowledge, we regularly organise webinars/lectures of top researchers and industry experts. Over the last few years, as many as forty webinars/lectures have been organised on varied topics to enhance the learning experience of students and faculty members on contemporary and emerging technological issues.

Recreational/Extra Curricular activities

The Institute has comprehensive facilities for outdoor sports and games. BIT hosts several extracurricular activities, necessary for overall healthy development of young mind. It has a beautiful sports complex with a modern stadium for track courts and field events. Well-equipped gymnasiums with modern multi-gym facilities are available. Further, a spacious new indoor badminton stadium, fine grounds and courts for football, hockey, cricket, kabaddi, kho-kho, basketball, tennis, volleyball, badminton, etc. are available. In addition, we have a well-trained coach and guide to support and encourage sports activities for the students.

There are large number of Student Societies and Clubs, which supports students to nurture their hobbies and other creative talents for relaxation and entertainment. These Societies and Clubs include:

Dramatics Society, UNESCO Club, News and Publication Society (NAPS), Bhartiya Sahitya Parishad (BSP), Highlanders Adventure Club (HAC), Amateur Radio Society (ARS), Music Club, Fine Arts Society, Photographic Society, Students Chapter of the Institute of Engineers (IE), Students Chapter of IEEE, Students Chapter of IEE, SPIC-MACAY, Nature Club, Audio Visual Educational Club (AVEC) and Indian Association of College Going Scientists (IACGS)

Drama competitions, music competitions and annual student festivals (ANKUR, BITOTSAV), are conducted on a regular basis. Several traditional festivals like Saraswati Puja and Viswakarma Puja are also celebrated with great devotion on campus. The students participate and organize "Guru ka Langar" yearly on the occasion of the birthday of Guru Govind Singh.

 The various Institute’s teams have regularly participate on several student festivals and competitions across all over the country, including those held at IT-BHU, various IIT’s, BITS Pilani, ISM Dhanbad, Rotary International, etc. BIT students were always appreciated and noticed by the national organizing society from their credible performance and they bagged several awards in past on various events including music, drama, fine arts, literary etc.

 The Institute has hosted various Inter-University meets and tournaments at the National and Zonal levels in athletics, tennis, basketball, badminton, volleyball, football and hockey. The Institute’s teams also visit other Universities to participate in such tournaments. It is worth mentioning that our students have won laurels at national and International events too.

Location and Accessibility

Birla Institute of Technology is located in a campus at Mesra, about 17 kilometers from Ranchi Railway Station, 25 kilometers from Ranchi Airport, 27 kilometers from Hatia Railway Station. Both Taxi and Autorickshaw are available from Ranchi/Hatia Railway/Bus Station/ Airport to BIT Mesra.
