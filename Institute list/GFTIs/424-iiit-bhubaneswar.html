About the Institute

International Institute of Information Technology, Bhubaneswar was established by the Government of Odisha in 2006. It is designed to be a premier institute of Information Technology and related areas. The Institute has been converted into a unitary university by the Government of Odisha on 20th Jan 2014.

Rules For Change Branch

Normally, only those students will be eligible for consideration of a change of branch, after the second semester, who have

o   completed all the common credits required in the first two semesters of their studies, in their first attempt;

o    obtained a CGPA of not less than 8.00  in first year
