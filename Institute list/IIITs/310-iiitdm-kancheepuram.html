About the Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design & Manufacturing
IIITDM Kancheepuram, Chennai-127

(An Institute of National Importance Fully Funded by Govt of India)

With thriving Indian economy, our manufacturing sector is more determined to compete with global giants. This process has enhanced customer expectations for quality products with cost advantage. On account of this changing scenario, our industry needs suitably trained manpower with skill sets appropriate for design, development and prototyping using modern IT tools to achieve quality and cost advantage. 

Keeping this in mind, the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing, Kancheepuram was set up as an academic institution of excellence that would facilitate and promote the competitive advantage of Indian products in global markets. In addition to offering conventional programs, this institute promotes inter-disciplinary education and research in the area of Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) encompassing design and manufacturing using state-of-the-art concepts, tools, processes and practices of the industry the world over. 

This Institute offers design and manufacturing-oriented engineering education and research programs. These programs integrate engineering design and manufacturing with information technology. The institute is located on a 51-acre beautiful campus on the outskirts of Chennai, off the Vandalur-Kelambakkam road. It is a fully residential campus with a separate hostel for boys and girls with a dining facility. The campus houses the academic block, the administrative block, the lecture hall complex and the laboratory block.

Academic Structure

Design-Centric Engineering Education

Academic programs being offered at IIITDM Kancheepuram are novel and differ from their conventional counterparts. The students of this institute are exposed to design thinking and manufacturing aspects involved in Computer, Electronics and Mechanical engineering, in addition to the regular courses of the institute. A stream of courses starting from the evolution of design, design visualization using digital tools, design realization in hobby workshops, and individual and team-based product design and development were introduced. The students emerge as product designers who can conceptualize and evaluate ideas, and make tangible products through more systematic deliberations and discussions with academicians from reputed institutions and industry stakeholders. The programs offered by the institute are as under:

B Tech (4 year Program)
B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with Major in AI
B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
B.Tech in Smart Manufacturing
B.Tech in Design Engineering
B.Tech in Engineering Physics

Dual Degree (5 year Program)
B.Tech and  M.Tech in Computer Science Engineering
B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering and M.Tech in Communication Systems
B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering and M.Tech in Microelectronics and VLSI Systems
B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering and M.Tech in AI and Robotics

Facilities

Infrastructure

Sophisticated academic, laboratory and hostel are available to meet the academic and campus life requirements

Department laboratories 

The departments (CSE, ECE, ME, SH, SIDI) have modern infrastructure with access to tools and simulation software. The laboratories include     High-performance computing, Product Design and Prototyping, VLSI and Microelectronics, Advanced Manufacturing, AI and Robotics etc.

Library

The institute library (Knowledge Plaza) plays an important role in providing quality information and services to the members besides committed to supporting the Institute’s mission. The Library uses an automated (Library and Information Management) software KOHA with Integrated RFID Technology. All the registered users can access the Institute library anywhere within the campus through LAN/WiFi.

The institute is one of the core members of the Indian National Digital Library in Engineering Sciences and Technology (INDEST) consortium and has subscribed to nearly 600 e-journals, conference proceedings, standards, viz IEL (IEEE & IEE) and Elsevier Science Direct Links.

https://iiitdm.ac.in/library

Health Center 

The medical facility inside the campus is operating 24X7 along with an ambulance. Specialized doctor visits in the evening hours. The Institute also has tie-ups with reputed hospitals to provide medical care. All the students are also covered under group medical as well as accidental insurance coverage

Student Life at Institute

Hostel Life

IIITDM Kancheepuram is known not only for its excellent academic programs but also for its vibrant campus life. The institute offers a welcoming and inclusive environment where students can explore their interests, build lasting friendships, and have fun. The campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities including well-stocked libraries, modern labs, sports grounds, and comfortable hostels. Apart from academic pursuits, students can engage in a wide range of extracurricular activities such as music, dance, theatre, and sports. There are also several clubs and societies catering to various interests such as coding, robotics, photography, and entrepreneurship. The institute hosts various cultural and technical fests throughout the year, providing students with ample opportunities to showcase their talents and learn from their peers. With its lively and enriching campus life, IIITDM Kancheepuram offers a holistic learning experience that goes beyond the classroom.
