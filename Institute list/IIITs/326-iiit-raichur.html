About the Institute

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Raichur (IIITR) was established in 2019 as a centre of academic excellence in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. 


Though being a budding institute, IIITR offers various facilities to students without compromising on quality. IIITR is an institute of national importance, formed under the Public Private Partnership model, with contributions from Ministry of Education, Government of India and the Government of Karnataka.


With its highly esteemed and able faculty, modern curriculum and a batch of bright-minded individuals passed out, IIITR is an abode of creativity and innovation, ready to show the world what it is capable of. The highly flexible curriculum forged by the esteemed faculty exuberates innovation and inventiveness. With core subjects being incorporated from the first semester itself, the students can finish their courses quite early and focus on developing projects and industry-relevant skills.

Academic Structure

BTech Academic Structure - IIIT Raichur

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Raichur (IIIT Raichur) is the latest IIIT established in 2019 under the public-private partnership (PPP) model. Located in Raichur, Karnataka, it currently operates from the campus of the Government Engineering College (GEC) in Yaramarus. Plans are underway for a permanent campus on a 75-acre site in Raichur in the upcoming year (tentatively)​.

IIIT Raichur offers the following three Bachelor of Technology (BTech) specializations, and all the programmes offered by the Institute shall be governed by the Ordinance:

    1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) 

    2. Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI & DS)

    3. Mathematics and Computing (M & C)

The academic programs are designed to provide a robust foundation in information technology and computer science, fostering a strong research culture among students. The institution also emphasizes continuous evaluation through its Fractal Academic System.

Structure:

    1. Duration: 4 years, divided into 8 semesters.
    2. Credit requirements: For each specialization, there is a minimum credit requirement. In order to qualify for a BTech Degree of the Institute, a student is required to complete the credit requirement as prescribed in the curriculum for a particular programme.
     3. CSE: 125 credits
           AI & DS: 126 credits
           M & C: 129 credits
    4. Core Subjects: 
          (i) CSE:  Algorithms, Data Structures, Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Databases, Programming Languages. 
          (ii) AI & DS: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Analytics, Natural Language Processing.
          (iii) M & C: The core subjects typically encompass a blend of mathematics, computer science, and their applications.These subjects provide a strong foundation in both mathematics and computer science, preparing students for diverse career paths in industry and academia​. The courses also provide a comprehensive foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of mathematics and computing

    5. Electives: A variety of electives available in advanced topics and interdisciplinary areas. Electives from one specialization can be opted in other specializations given that timetable doesn’t clash and with a prior approval form the concerned department/faculty. 

    6.  Internships: The curriculum has flexible semester-long and summer internships as part of its course work. It gives students ample opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios. They help in developing professional skills such as teamwork, communication, and problem-solving, while also offering valuable industry exposure and networking opportunities. This experience enhances employability by making students more attractive to potential employers, demonstrating their readiness to enter the workforce with hands-on experience.

B.Tech Honors

IIIT Raichur has provision for an Honors program (for all the three specializations mentioned above) that is designed to challenge the brighter and more ambitious students, without burdening an average student. 

Some salient features are listed below:

● A student can opt for Honors after the completion of the second year.

● The student should have a CGPA >= 8.0 (without any backlog) at the end of the fourth

semester.

● The student must complete an additional 12 discipline credits.

● The student should have CGPA >= 8.0 (without any backlog) at the end of the eighth

semester and should not have any backlog throughout the B.Tech course

Curriculum Highlights: The curriculum at IIIT Raichur is designed to provide a comprehensive knowledge while keeping in mind the requirements of the industry and the nation. 

In a nutshell:

    i. First Year: Broad courses from engineering, science, and liberal arts.

    ii. Second Year: Core engineering and interdisciplinary subjects.

    iii. Third Year: Specialization electives and research projects.

iv. Fourth Year: Project-heavy, focusing on research and industry-oriented projects.

Unique Features:

    1. Strong Foundational Core: The curriculum includes a broad-based foundation in the initial years, covering essential subjects such as mathematics, basic sciences, and introductory engineering courses. This ensures that students develop a strong grasp of fundamental concepts before moving on to more specialized topics.

    2. Specialization and Advanced Topics_: In the later years, the curriculum offers specialized courses and advanced courses along with the core courses. This includes courses like machine learning, deep learning, data analytics, and other advanced topics that are crucial for modern technological applications. This structure allows students to gain in-depth knowledge and expertise in their chosen fields.


    3. Fractal Academic System_: IIIT Raichur follows a Fractal Academic System, which emphasizes continuous evaluation and modular courses. This system is designed to provide flexibility and enable students to learn at their own pace, adapting the course load to their individual needs and interests. Such an approach can enhance the learning experience by reducing stress and allowing for more personalized education paths.


    4. Research and Practical Exposure_: The curriculum encourages research and practical exposure through projects, internships, and industry collaborations. This hands-on experience is vital for students to apply their theoretical knowledge to real-world problems and develop practical skills that are highly valued by employers.


    5. Interdisciplinary Learning_: The interdisciplinary approach in the curriculum allows students to explore and integrate knowledge from various fields. This not only broadens their understanding but also fosters innovative thinking and problem-solving skills, preparing them for the complex challenges of the tech industry. The institute also offers courses related to community development and engagement in collaboration with Swami Vivekananda Youth Movement (SVYM).


    6. Placement and Career Opportunities_: The curriculum’s alignment with industry needs. Students are well-prepared for successful careers in technology and engineering fields​.

    Overall, the curriculum at IIIT Raichur is robust, flexible, and aligned with contemporary technological advancements. It provides students with a solid foundation, specialized knowledge, practical skills, and ample opportunities for research and industry engagement. This comprehensive educational framework is designed to produce well-rounded graduates equipped to meet the demands of the evolving tech landscape.

Student Life at Institute

The student family of IIITR consists of competitive coders, web-development enthusiasts, eager learners and diligent scholars. They have been actively involved in various technical and cultural club activities of IIIT Raichur. At the same time, the students have founded and set up numerous clubs to further facilitate collaborative learning and introspective growth. 


To address the needs of the students related to the regular academic coursework at IIIT Raichur, the library at IIITR contains multiple copies of standard textbooks. The students are actively encouraged to make optimal use of the library by borrowing the required textbooks and reference books from the library to complement their classroom learning in the best possible way. The library at IIIT Raichur currently accommodates at least 30 readers at any given point of time during office hours.


IIITR has a common Dining facility for all its students inside the campus premises. Both north Indian and south Indian varieties are served in the dining hall. Student representatives and faculty in-charges regularly inspect the quality of food.


IIITR also offers an adequate hostel facility with already furnished rooms that are regularly maintained. The hostel facilities at IIITR are very good.


Thus, on an overall note, IIIT Raichur is a wonderful place to explore, learn, and show what you are.
