About the Institute

Dear prospective student, 

 

A Warm Welcome to the Vibrant and Dynamic Academic Community of IIIT Kottayam!

IIIT Kottayam emerges as a cornerstone, dedicated to fostering the next generation of visionaries and problem solvers in Computer Science, Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, Electronics and Communication and related fields. Founded in 2015 with a vision of "Generating knowledge for the future", IIITK has since evolved into a dynamic hub where cutting-edge research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and hands-on learning converge to shape the future of IT and related domains. The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam at Valavoor, Pala, Kerala is one among the IIITs that have been established as "Institutions of National Importance" by Ministry of Education, Govt. of India under the ambit of IIIT (PPP) Act 2017. IIIT Kottayam campus is situated at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam - a pictorial location, spread over in 53 acres of land - allotted by Govt. of Kerala in the southern part of the state commonly referred to as God's Own Country. The Institute is functioning from the permanent campus from 2019 and currently has a student strength of 1675.

The institute offers a diverse array of courses spanning fields such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Cyber Security, AI and Data Science ensuring students access comprehensive education tailored to their career aspirations. IIIT Kottayam offers B.Tech-CSE, B.Tech-Cyber Security, B.Tech-AI &DS, B.Tech-ECE, M.Tech for Working Professionals in AI &DS, Cyber Security, Big Data and ML, Integrated M.Tech for Working Professionals and PhD in CSE, ECE and Mathematics. The faculty at our institute embodies a rare blend of academic prowess, industry experience, and dedication to student growth. With a vast array of achievements, including publications in top-tier journals, patents, and accolades, they bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom.

IIIT Kottayam has a strong track record of placement, with graduates being recruited by leading companies in the IT industry, both in India and abroad. The institute has a dedicated placement cell that facilitate interactions between students and potential employers through campus recruitment drives, internships, and industry visits. IIIT Kottayam has partnerships with industry associations, research organizations, and start-ups to provide students with opportunities for hands-on experience and exposure to the latest trends and technologies in the IT and related sectors. Some of the key achievements of the Institute include a fully established campus within a short span of 10 years, more than 40 Research papers from UG students and 400 + research papers from Faculty, 03 Incubation centres- Atal Incubation Centre (NITI Ayog) , Two MSME centres, GYAN Lab jointly with Industry, more than 12 Active Academic MoUs including TUM Germany, University of Glasgow, International Consultancy from British Council, Volkswagen Foundation and Mozilla Foundation, more than 320 Research Proposals and 40 Cr + sectioned projects, M.Tech for Working Professionals with a student strength of 218 in May 2025, PhD Strength of 118 ( May 2025) with 33 external sponsorships, more than 4 +Cr Industrial consultancy for faculty and 12 + Hackathon winners from student community.

From its modest inception, the institute has undergone a remarkable journey of growth and evolution, surpassing all expectations and emerging as a beacon of excellence in education. Through visionary leadership, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, the institute has garnered recognition as a beacon of academic excellence, transforming lives and shaping the future of generations to come.

The following documents are required to be produced (Original + Attested copy) at the time of admission.          
1. Provisional seat allocation/admission letter, downloaded from JoSAA/DASA website.
2. Document verification completion and seat acceptance letter, issued by JoSAA/CSAB/DASA
3. Original admit card of JEE (Main) 2025
4. Score card of JEE (main) 2025
5. 10th standard / Secondary School Certificate
6. Medical examination report produced at the time of counselling
7. a) Mark sheet/grade card of the qualifying examination (Class 12)
   b) Pass certificate of the qualifying examination (Class 12)
8. School Leaving/ Transfer Certificate from the institution last attended.
9. Character and Conduct certificate from the institution last attended. If Class 12 year of passing is 2023 or 2024, then Character and Conduct certificate endorsed by any gazetted officer.
10. Category certificate (GEN-EWS/OBC-NCL/SC/ST/PWD), if applicable, as per Government of India format, available on the JoSAA/CSAB-2025 website, issued by the competent authority
11. Passport size photographs - 3 Nos.
12. Receipt of fee paid at SBI Fee Collect (the amount which is due to the institute at the time of admission).
13. Copy of Aadhaar Card of the Candidate
14. Vaccination Proof for Chickenpox (Those who were not infected earlier)
15. Copy of the front page of the student's Bank Passbook with account details.
16. Copy of the proof of permanent address.
17. APAAR/ABC ID
Note: Any change in the name of the candidate from the name as given in Class X Certificate should be accompanied by a Notary approved legal name change proof document.

Note 1: Candidates infected by chicken pox earlier -- A Self-declaration stating the period of infection with chicken pox along with the signatures of student and parent needs to be submitted.

Note 2: All students must have a laptop at the time of admission with a minimum configuration of i5/i7 processor or equivalent with at least 8 GB RAM, and Linux Installation (UBUNTU 22.04 or higher preferred).

Facilities

All the academic activities of IIITK are conducted in the permanent campus at Valavoor. So students need to be joining and reporting at this permanent campus only.

Hostel: Hostel Facility is available for Boys and Girls students. Students run the cooperative mess based on their menu in hostels. The mess is compulsory for all students. The girl students will be accommodated in the hostel within the campus. All the boy students of the first year will be accommodated (3 per room) in different rented hostels outside the campus (within 03 to 08 KMs) and the Institute will provide conveyance (to and fro) free of cost. All boy students will be accommodated inside the campus hostels / rented hostels within walkable distance from the second year onwards. This is a policy decision, and binding on all students opting the Institute. Hostels are supported with generator facility upto 11 PM.

Medical Facility: The Medical Centre at our institute places a strong emphasis on the wellness of students and staff, providing tailored services to meet their specific needs. The services include regular consultations for common ailments and comprehensive diagnostic services including laboratory tests (in collaboration with Rajiv Gandhi Centre for Biotechnology, Thiruvananthapuram). We have highly qualified medical professionals including doctors, nurses and laboratory technicians. Our team provides empathetic and patient-centered approach to all students and staff members, ensuring a supportive and comforting environment. The general facilities include a consultation room, procedure room, a comfortable waiting area, separate male and female ward, isolation room and a sample collection centre. The medical centre is also equipped with advanced diagnostic and support facilities like ECG machine, Autoclave, UV Sterilizer, Steamer, Infrared massager, Glucometer, Otoscope, Oxygen concentrator, Nebulizer, Pulse Oximeter, etc.  The medical centre also offers 24/7 emergency services. The Medical Centre at our institute has established strong partnerships with nearby hospitals like Mar Sleeva Medicity Pala and Marian Medical Centre Pala to enhance the quality of healthcare services and ensure comprehensive care for our community. Initiatives like health camps, awareness programs and blood donation camps are conducted regularly with the support of these hospitals. The institute has also employed a full-time counselling psychologist to support the mental and emotional well-being of students, staff, and the broader community.

State of the art Labs/Infrastructures: IIIT Kottayam continues constructing buildings and establishing research labs based on the requirement of faculty/industries. Construction of several research labs such as IoT Cloud research lab, Computer Networking lab, 3D printing design lab, Cyber Lab, Gyaan Lab, FACTS-H Lab, Intelligent Integrated Circuits and Systems (I2CS) Lab and so forth is in progress at IIIT Kottayam for improving the practical knowledge of students.  Selective students could visit leading industries (including AIM-Aayog, Intel, CDAC, NIWE) and international Universities such as University of Innsbruck, Austria, University of Klagenfurt Austria, Technical University Munich Germany and so forth where our faculties are collaborated with research ideas.

IIIT Kottayam is driving impactful research in 6G, IoT, UAV security, and High-Performance Computing (HPC) through prestigious global collaborations and funded projects. The institute is leading the ASEAN DST-funded HPC project in partnership with AIT Thailand, Universiti Teknologi Brunei, Cambodia Public University, and other institutions, advancing computational research for disaster management and climate adaptation. Additionally, IIIT Kottayam is spearheading projects on 5G, 6G, and IoT security in collaboration with the University of Glasgow, supported by MeitY, EPSRC UK, UKI-FNI, and the British Council.

The British Council’s Going Global Partnership Programme supports a unique collaborative project between IIIT Kottayam and the University of Glasgow, UK, focused on IoT for Intelligent Systems, with a funding of ₹15 lakhs. This initiative emphasizes the development and delivery of a joint Transnational Education (TNE) program, aiming to provide a world-class learning experience for undergraduate students at both institutions. The academic teams, including the Director of IIIT Kottayam and faculty members from the University of Glasgow, worked closely to design a comprehensive course curriculum that integrates both theoretical foundations and practical applications of IoT technologies. The course, offered simultaneously at both institutions, covers critical topics such as IoT architecture, sensor networks, data analytics, and smart system design, ensuring that students receive a robust education aligned with global industry standards. A distinctive feature of the program is its strong practical component, where students engage in hands-on projects and real-world problem-solving. Through virtual labs, interactive workshops, and collaborative assignments, students from both institutions work together on innovative IoT-based solutions for smart environments. To further enhance the learning experience, the program includes faculty and student exchanges, with visits from IIIT Kottayam faculty to the University of Glasgow and vice versa. These visits facilitate guest lectures, workshops, and practical training sessions, providing students with invaluable exposure to international academic practices and fostering a collaborative learning environment. This joint effort not only enriches the educational experience but also strengthens research collaborations, setting a precedent for future TNE initiatives. By aligning their educational strategies and leveraging their combined expertise, IIIT Kottayam and the University of Glasgow aim to create a model program that equips students with the skills and knowledge to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of IoT.

IIIT Kottayam has signed MOU’s with various international players such as University of North Dakota, USA, Offenburg University of Applied Sciences, Germany, University of Glasgow, United Kingdom, University of Klagenfurt, Austria, Technical University of Munich, Germany, National Chung Cheng University, Taiwan, University of Agder, Norway, University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, to promote research, internships and knowledge sharing.

To facilitate remote access, services like the Knimbus Digital Library and the INFED authentication system are in place. Further, the Library supports research and academic development through platforms like the Indian Research Information Network System (IRINS) and ShodhGanga. With a focus on accessibility, resourcefulness, and innovation, the Central Library remains committed to fostering a vibrant environment of learning and inquiry for the IIIT Kottayam community.

Student Life at Institute

All our efforts are only focusing on students to get a vibrant and creative life at par with other Institutions of National Importance within the constraints of the ongoing construction activities of the second phase of infrastructure development.

Life at IIIT Kottayam: IIIT Kottayam ensures a 100 percent anti-ragging campus. The newcomers of this institute are well-treated without inequity and gender bias. We have an active mentoring system and each student will get faculty mentors. Faculty mentors always keep in touch with parents also. We always try our level best to ensure a FEEL-AT-HOME environment during their stay at IIIT Kottayam (right from their initial stay at the campus). A high-speed internet connection with bandwidth of 6 Gbps with WiFi access through NKN network and the digital library connections / IEEE subscriptions keep motivating the IIIT Kottayam students for developing innovative solutions during their stay.

Skill Development: One of the main attractions of IIITK is we are having several forums for students to develop their skills and talents.  In addition to the aid offered by our teachers, our very own committed technical club, Beta Labs, offers them crucial student-level assistance. Last year our IIITK family expanded by more than 400 students, and our technological activities also advanced significantly. We have IEEE student branch, ACM student chapter, Sports club, Cultural club ( WildBeats), Dance and Music Club, Drama Club, Finance club, Literary club, Coding Club, Cyber Security Club, and so forth have enabled most of the students to impart their leadership skills at IIIT Kottayam. 

Location Benefits: IIIT Kottayam is located in the central part of Kerala, phrased as God’s Own Country. Kerala is a state with 100 percent literacy. Those who are visiting here understand that we maintained Nature's natural slope even in the construction activities.  It is a business hub for trading spices and is an industrial sector for rubber industries, including MRF, Apollo, etc. IIIT Kottayam is located in an ideal place where students could reach Trivandrum, Kochi, or Bangalore for establishing business and research developments. Please visit www.iiitkottayam.ac.in for further information.

The MSME incubation centre is also sanctioned to the Institute. IIIT Kottayam, thus, helps students to continue championing in their career and attain goals with an entrepreneurial support. It provides tens of thousands of opportunities for them to interact with industrial experts, including MSMEs to MNCs. Recently one of the student start-up namely,”unifindss”,has officially recognized by MSME,GOI.

Visit http://icentre.iiitkottayam.ac.in for further information.

Placements / Internships: IIIT Kottayam has a sound internship and placement programme. Leading industries keep lining up for conducting interviews and placement/internship drives to our students. In fact, the students of IIIT Kottayam could easily attract industries for pursuing internships during their summer/winter vacations (4 months per year). Most often, these internships do lead to their future placement offers.  In the previous years, our students have attracted stipends during internships ranging from Rs.15000 to Rs.50000/- per month from leading industries, including Bosch, TCS, NIWE, US Technologies, IBM, VOLVO, HARMAN, and so forth. Top companies like Amazon and Google offer ₹1.25 lakhs in internship stipends. The institute even facilitates internships abroad, with past opportunities at JP Morgan Chase Glasgow and Volvo Sweden. The placement cell is responsible for campus Internships at IIIT Kottayam. The placement cell establishes and maintains relationships with various industries and organizations, cultivating partnerships and collaborations to understand their hiring needs, trends, and requirements.

The placement cell establishes and maintains a strong network of alumni who can contribute to the betterment of the process. The role of a placement cell is crucial in bridging the gap between academia and industry, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the professional world. We are well-equipped with all the necessary facilities for the Internship process. Looking at placements, this year saw a record-breaking 219 total offers with the highest domestic package reaching ₹50 lakhs per annum and an average of ₹12 lakhs per annum. The international scene was impressive too, with the highest offer reaching ₹23 lakhs per annum and a third-year student even receiving a pre-placement offer (PPO) of a remarkable ₹50 lakhs per annum.

Please visit http://www.iiitkottayam.ac.in/#!/placement

IIIT Kottayam has positioned itself as a leading institution in India by fostering a robust culture of innovation and establishing strong industry connections. The institute has secured numerous industrial projects funded by multinational companies such as TCS, IBM, Infosys, Hewlett Packard, NeST, UniSys, Microsoft, HPC Solutions, Mozilla Foundations, IonQ USA, Komhar Services etc. and research projects funded by government agencies such as DST, MeitY, SERB, MSME, ANRF and other international funding bodies such as IGSTC, DST-DAAD, EPSRC, UKIERI.  This extensive collaboration includes partnerships with major IT companies and organizations, ensuring that the research conducted is both cutting-edge and relevant to industry needs. Furthermore, IIIT Kottayam supports innovation through its incubation centers, such as the MSME Incubation Centre, AIC Incubation Centre (NITI Aayog) and innovation centre - Gyaan Innovation Lab, which have secured several start-up funding proposals and have successfully incubated multiple start-up companies. We designed India’s first 5G-enabled 3D scanner and visualization system, winning the Vimarsh 5G Hackathon organized by the Ministry of Home Affairs in association with BPR&D and TCoE.  We developed a web-based solution for crime scene recreation for Kerala Police Cyberdome. IIIT Kottayam  won the Best Exhibitee Award at India Mobile Congress, Asia’s largest tech fest, for showcasing our crime scene recreation system using the metaverse. The Gyaan Lab received  received the Special Innovation Award from KSCSTE for developing a novel egg grading system.It also developed  technology solutions for Ayurvedic practitioners in collaboration with ITRA Jamnagar and Vaidyaratnam called “e-nasya”. Our crime scene recreation system was featured in Interpol’s white paper.

To cultivate a strong programming and technical culture, the institute has established various student chapters and coding clubs, including GeeksForGeeks, CodeChef, Coding Ninjas, and Google Developer Student Club. These clubs regularly organize events, webinars, and competitions to enhance students' coding skills and prepare them for global competitions. Additionally, IIIT Kottayam's curriculum is designed to emphasize programming languages, data structures, and computer algorithms in the initial semesters, providing students with a competitive edge.

The institute also encourages students to engage in projects that address real-world problems, often in collaboration with industry partners. This hands-on approach helps students develop a research-oriented problem-solving attitude. IIIT Kottayam hosts several research groups in areas such as Data Science, Big Data & Machine Learning, Cyber Security, IoT, and more. These groups comprise faculty members, students, alumni, industry experts, and international collaborators, enhancing the institute's research output and global presence. Through these initiatives, IIIT Kottayam ensures that its students are well-equipped with the knowledge and experience needed to excel in the IT industry and beyond.

The curriculum consists of courses that are jointly offered by industry experts and faculty of IIIT Kottayam. Our alumni network strengthens the existing bondage between our ongoing students and industries. Alumni are committed to assist our students for placements, internships, and organizing knowledge-transfer programmes. Several events are often jointly organized with our prestigious alumni to promote the word-of-mouth marketing synergies of the Institute. In addition, they are serving as mentors to potential startups of our incubation centers which remain as entrepreneurial promotion activities.
