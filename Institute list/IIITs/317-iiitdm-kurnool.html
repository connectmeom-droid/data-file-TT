About the Institute


Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh
An Institute of National Importance, under the Ministry of Education (MoE), Govt. of India


_Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM), Kurnool is an autonomous institute established in 2015 and is one of the FIVE Centrally funded IIITs by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India.

Currently, this Institute is offering 4 B. Tech programmes in Computer Science and Engineering with (Sanctioned Intake:70), Electronics and Communication Engineering with (Sanctioned Intake:60), Mechanical Engineering (Sanctioned Intake:60) and Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (Sanctioned Intake:60) intake of students and 3 Dual degree programs in Computer Science and Engineering (5 Years, B. Tech. & M. Tech. Dual Degree-Sanctioned Intake: 20), Electronics and Communication Engineering (5 Years, B. Tech. & M. Tech. Dual Degree-Sanctioned Intake: 20), Mechanical Engineering (5 Years, B. Tech. & M. Tech. Dual Degree-Sanctioned Intake: 20). Apart from these programmes, IIITDM Kurnool is also offering 7 M. Tech programmes and Ph. D. in all the departments. For details about the curriculum and syllabus of subjects being taught for the programmes are available at the corresponding department webpages.

The Government of Andhra Pradesh has allocated approximately 152 acres of land to build the campus at Kurnool. Our Institute is located at Jagannthagattu, Dinnidevarapadu Village. The nearest Railway station is Kurnool City and is located at about 12 km from the Institute.  The nearest airport is Kurnool Airport, officially known as Uyyalawada Narasimha Reddy Airport, which is a greenfield airport at Orvakal, Kurnool district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. The distance from Airport to the Institute is approximately 20km. It is situated on National Highway 40.

 The Institute is running in permanent buildings that were developed in July 2019. Presently, the institute has an academic wing, an administrative wing, lecture halls, seminar halls, and laboratory blocks along with hostels and mess block for 1200 students.

Facilities

1. Hostel accommodation: Presently, the Institute is equipped with sufficient hostels for Boys and Girls separately, with 1200 capacity.

2. Internet Facility: We have a WiFi-enabled academic area with the Internet facility being provided in every lab and across the classrooms.

3. Seminar Halls: The Institute has 4  AC Seminar Halls. These are being used for conducting meetings, seminars, workshops, and classes.

4. Library: The Central Library of the institute has a collection of more than 2200 books. It has a sufficient number of copies of textbooks and reference books for the courses being offered. The Central Library also subscribes to more than 23 journals, magazines, and newspapers.

5. Laboratory facilities: The Institute has air-conditioned, specialized, with high-end equipment in each laboratory. Labs have advanced testing and controlling equipment like Computer Labs with high-end computing facilities, Workshops, manufacturing laboratory, Real-time Spectrum analyzer, DAC Hardware, Isolated mixed domain oscilloscope, EMC probe set, Line Impedance stabilization network LISN, Electro pneumatics ith PLC, High Voltage Probe. Apart from that, labs also have software like MATLAB, VIVADO, CATIA V5, ANSYS, LAB VIEW, CADENCE, AUTO CAD, and SKETCHBOOK PRO.

6. Canteen: The institute has a cafeteria which serves different items at very reasonable rates.

Student Life at Institute

IIITDM Kurnool strives to provide a conducive environment to the students so that after completion of the course they are industry ready. In this regard, we extend academic help to students beyond the regular class teaching. We have signed MOU with CodeTantra to provide online support for students to enhance their coding skills. Different departments are also in the process of establishing technical associations. These associations will be responsible for organizing technical activities in collaboration with IEEE, ACM, ISTE, IEI, and CSI. To reduce the boredom arising out of rigorous academic activities, the institute encourages students to pursue extra-curricular activities.  The institute also encourages students for participation in cultural and sports events within and outside the institute. In the past, students have participated in the inter IIITs Sports Meet. SOLASKA a Techno-cultural fest is being organized every year at the institute for students to showcase their talent.

Training & Placement

Objective: The objective of the Internship and Placement Cell is to orient the outgoing engineering students equipped with the Industry skills and help them to accomplish their anticipated employment and career goals.

Functions:

1. To nurture the Industry-Institute-Interaction, collaborate as a partner with various industries to identify suitable opportunities for the students of the Institute for getting exposure to current trends in technology and business processes.

2. To establish a state of the art in house training programs in order to enhance the employability skills of the students.

3. Communicate with various reputed organizations/companies to conduct Internship and Placement drives, to fulfill the career aspirations of the students.

4. Gathers the feedback on curriculum improvement from the collaborative organizations/companies to ensure that our student become Quality Engineers for Successful Future.

5. Ensure 100% placement of the students.
