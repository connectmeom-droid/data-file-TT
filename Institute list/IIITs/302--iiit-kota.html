About the Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology Kota is a joint venture of the Ministry of Education, Government of India, Rajasthan Government with Industries in Not for profit Public-Private Partnership (N-PPP) model. Institute has very supportive and active Industry partners namely, CAIRN India Ltd., VAKRANGI Software, GENPACT and NEI Ltd. 

IIIT Kota was established in the year 2013 and is mentored by MNIT Jaipur. The institute has shifted to its permanent campus at Kota in August 2023. Since August 2024, all the classes are being held in its permanent campus only. IIIT Kota has hostels for both boys and girls with mess facility. All other necessary facilities like medical are also provided.

For more details, please visit https://iiitkota.ac.in/

Academic Structure

The institute offers Bachelor of Technology (B. Tech.) program in  the following three streams: 

1. Computer Science and Engineering
2. Electronics and Communication Engineering 
3. Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering 

In general, the duration of B. Tech. program is 8-semesters (4-years).  
For complete details of programs offered, please visit https://iiitkota.ac.in/department

Facilities

The institute has academic building for all academic and administrative works. Classes and labs are conducted in the academic building.
