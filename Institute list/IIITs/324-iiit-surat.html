About the Institute

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Surat (IIIT Surat), established in 2017 under the Ministry of Education's Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model, stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education. Recognized as an Institute of National Importance, IIIT Surat is a collaborative endeavour involving the Government of India, the Government of Gujarat and industry partners such as Gujarat Narmada Valley Fertilizers & Chemicals (GNFC), Gujarat Gas Ltd. (GGL), and Gujarat Informatics Ltd. (GIL). Since September 2022, the Institute has been operating from its own academic facility on a 28.71-acre campus in Kholvad, Kamrej, Surat.

IIIT Surat offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degrees in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE). Additionally, two new programmes are introduced in Computer Science and Engineering with specializations in AI-ML and Cyber Security. Admissions are based on performance in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main, with seat allocation managed by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) and the Central Seat Allocation Board (CSAB). The institute expanded its academic horizon by introducing the Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) program in 2022 and Master of Technology (M.Tech.) program in 2025.

IIIT Surat boasts a strategic location near National Highway No. 48, facilitating connectivity between Mumbai and New Delhi. The institute's infrastructure includes modern classrooms equipped with audio-visual aids, a well-stocked library, and state-of-the-art laboratories specializing in areas such as Programming, Networking, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, and Robotics. IIIT Surat has an outstanding placement record, with students securing high-paying jobs in top-tier companies, startups, and research organizations.

The institute places a strong emphasis on research and development, engaging in partnership with various agencies and industries. Collaborations with global entities like ISRO, MediaTek Inc., Taiwan, ITMO University, Russia, and Bosch Global Software Technology underscore its commitment to integrating academic learning with practical industry applications.

Under the leadership of Director Rajeev Shorey, IIIT Surat is dedicated to fostering an environment where academic excellence, innovative research, and robust industry collaborations converge. The institute aims to prepare its students to be continuous learners and leaders in an ever-evolving global landscape.

In summary, IIIT Surat represents a dynamic fusion of academic excellence, innovative research, and industry partnership, positioning itself as a pivotal institution in shaping the future of Information Technology education in India.” Please visit the website www.iiitsurat.ac.in for more details.

Facilities

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Surat (IIIT Surat) is a premier technical institute offering B.Tech., M.Tech., and Ph.D. programs with a focus on AI, Cybersecurity, Data Science, Semiconductors and IoT, backed by state-of-the-art facilities including smart classrooms, advanced computing labs (AI/ML, IoT, HPC), library, and support for startups. The institute features hostels, indoor/outdoor sport, a medical centre, and a vibrant student life with hackathons and cultural events.

Student Life at Institute

Student Life at IIIT Surat is a vibrant blend of academics, innovation, and cultural engagement, offering a dynamic environment through tech clubs like GDSC (Google Developer Student Clubs) and LCS (Learn Code Solve) for coding and hackathons, Ruminate E-Cell for entrepreneurship, and MARC Robotics Club for hands-on projects in IoT and automation. The campus buzzes with cultural festivities like SARAS (celebrating Indian heritage) and Spring Fiesta, an annual festival featuring competitions in dance, drama, and tech, alongside sports tournaments organized by the Indominus Club. With initiatives like Yuva Sangam fostering national integration and SSIP-funded startups, students enjoy holistic growth, supported by a thriving ecosystem of workshops, industry collaborations, and a tight-knit community that balances rigorous academics with creativity and leadership opportunities.

Financial Assistance

Scholarships offered by the Central Government (via the National Scholarship Portal) and respective State Governments are available to eligible students in accordance with prevailing regulations. To benefit from these opportunities, students must submit the prescribed applications to the appropriate authorities. IIIT-Surat ensures a seamless scholarship experience through dedicated staff who provide guidance and support throughout the entire process.

Training & Placement

Training and Placements at IIIT Surat are driven by a robust ecosystem that bridges academia and industry, ensuring students excel in their careers. The institute boasts an impressive highest package of ₹74 LPA (2025) and an average of ₹14 LPA, with top recruiters like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Goldman Sachs, and NVIDIA actively participating. The Training and Placement Cell (T&P) facilitates 6-month industry internships, pre-placement talks, and skill-building workshops, while the curriculum integrates emerging technologies like AI, ML, and cybersecurity. With 70%+ placement rates and alumni securing roles in Fortune 500 companies or pursuing higher studies at premier global universities, IIIT Surat empowers students through personalized mentorship, mock interviews, and strong corporate ties, making it a launchpad for thriving careers in tech and beyond. More information on T&P activities is available at: https://www.iiitsurat.ac.in/placement_home.php

Industry And Alumni Relations

Industry and Alumni Relations at IIIT Surat fosters strong collaborations between academia and corporate partners through MoUs with leading organizations like MEDIATEK, BOSCH, and ISRO, facilitating joint research, internships, and curriculum development. The institute actively engages its alumni network—spanning top tech firms and global universities—for mentorship, guest lectures, and placement opportunities. Initiatives like industry-sponsored labs, hackathons, and the Student Startup Innovation Policy (SSIP 2.0) further strengthen ties, ensuring students gain real-world exposure while companies access skilled talent, creating a symbiotic ecosystem for innovation and career growth.

Recreational/Extra Curricular activities

Recreational & Extra-Curricular Activities at IIIT Surat offer a vibrant campus life through diverse clubs like Indominus (sports/gaming), SARAS (cultural events), and MARC (robotics), along with annual festivals like Spring Fiesta featuring tech, arts, and sports competitions. The institute promotes holistic development with Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) hackathons, Yuva Sangam cultural exchanges, and E-cell startup initiatives, ensuring students balance academics with creativity, teamwork, and leadership. Facilities include sports grounds, music/dance studios, and an Amphitheatre, fostering a dynamic environment for talent showcase and relaxation.

Location and Accessibility

Surat is a city of diamond and textile industries and well-known for its top ranking among the clean cities in India. The academic campus of the Institute is near the National Highway No.48 connecting New Delhi and Mumbai. The new campus is at a distance of 15 km from the Surat Railway Station and Interstate Bus Terminus, and at a distance of 32 km from Surat Airport. Different parts of the city of Surat are well-connected with BRTS facilities.
