About the Institute

Pandit Dwarka Prasad Mishra

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing, Jabalpur
 (An Institute of National Importance Fully Funded by Govt of India)

 

Pandit Dwarka Prasad Mishra Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing, Jabalpur (PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur) was established by the Ministry of Education (erstwhile Ministry of Human Resource Development), Government of India in January 2005 with an aim to promote excellence in the field of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing. It was envisaged that the new Institute would serve as an inter-disciplinary institution for education and research by keeping the concept of Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) in mind. The Institute is being developed and nurtured under the India-Japan partnership as a unique Institute of its kind with a focus on IT enabled Design and Manufacturing. 

The charter of PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur is enunciated as:

i) To emerge amongst the foremost institutions in Information Technology (IT) enabled Design and Manufacturing and allied fields of knowledge;

ii) To advance new knowledge and innovation in IT enabled Design and Manufacturing allied fields to empower the nation to the forefront from the global perspective;

iii) To develop competent and capable youth imbued with the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship with the social and environmental orientation to meet the knowledge needs of the country and provide global leadership in IT enabled Design and Manufacturing and allied fields.

The Vision and mission of the Institute are

i) Vision

PDPM Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM), Jabalpur shall emerge as a Global Knowledge Hub for quality research and teaching under the broad area of IT enabled Design and Manufacturing through cross-disciplinary, innovative, futuristic and dynamic approaches.

ii) Mission

To create an environment of high-quality education and research which:

  a. Provides maximal opportunities for intellectual and creative development.

  b. Provides exposure to solve real life problems through interdisciplinary approaches.

  c. Encourage students to learn through inquiry with special emphasis on hands on experience , “learning through doing”.

  d. Eventually to create a future generation for the building of the Nation.

To meet the Vision of the Institute, key features of the institute are:

·   Admission: The Institute admits meritorious students through National Eligibility tests such as JEE, UCEED, GATE, CEED, NET, etc.

·   Faculty: The faculty of the Institute is well-qualified (holds Ph.D. degree from premier Institutions) with proven academic and research record.

·   Curriculum: The Institute curriculum is designed to inculcate multi-disciplinary learning with focus on developing innovativeness and research among the students.

·   Research: The Institute promotes research in pure and applied research areas for the benefit of the society. Faculty undertake research projects sponsored by various funding agencies such as: DST, DRDO, ISRO, CSIR, DHI, MHRD, NCERT as well as prominent Industries. The Institute promotes the filing of IPR, Technology Transfer and Commercialization in the interest of the Nation.

·   Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship (IIE): The Institute promotes Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship (IIE) ecosystem through Technology Business Incubation (TBI) cell. It facilitates student startups on campus to promote entrepreneurship.

·   Service to Society (S2S): The Institute provides services to the society by carrying out research in the area for the benefit of the society. In addition, the Institute runs Jagriti Club, which provides education and learning to the rural children in and around the campus. The students of the Institute under the mentorship of the faculty manage the entire affair.

Rules For Change Branch

i)       A student may be allowed change of branch/ discipline on the basis of her/his academic performance, subject to strength constraints of the disciplines. Change of branch/ disciplineisaprivilegeandnotarightandisawardednormallytomeritorious students only. Reservation policy will be applicable as per GOI rules for vacant seats.

ii)     Application for the change of branch/ discipline should be made at the end of second semester of BTech programme to the Dean Academic.

iii)   Change of Branch/ discipline will be done against the vacant seats and will be based purely on merit basis (CPI of the student towards the end of the second semester).

iv)   The strength of the students in a discipline shall not exceed the larger of its existing and sanctioned strengths or fall below 60% of its sanctioned strength as a result of branch/ discipline change.

v)      Final approval for Change of Discipline will be given by Dean (Academic).

vi)   Top 5 students of the entire B.Tech. batch shall be eligible to change their branch of their choice above the sanctioned strength.

Student Life at Institute

Students' Gymkhana

Students’ Gymkhana is constituted to evolve a disciplined self-governance for carrying out various extracurricular in-campus activities and to establish a responsible and accountable student body. Students’ Gymkhana is governed by Student Senate which is constituted in democratic way through elections among each discipline and batch of the students. Student Senate members are elected through direct ballot voting, every year in the first week of January. Every registered student of the Institute is by-default member of student gymkhana and would have right to vote. All members of the student gymkhana who have their CPI >= 6.5 will be eligible to make their candidature for the Student Senate. The Students’ Senate has 2-3 representatives from each class. There are around 40 members in the Student Senate. Various gymkhana clubs that are part of Cultural, Sports and Technical, work under Students gymkhana. Club coordinators and co-coordinators will be selected based on their skill and past performance in the Institute by the members of that club and duly ratified by the Student Senate. All members of the student gymkhana who have their CPI>= 6.0 are eligible to become mentors, coordinators, co-coordinators for various clubs.

Name of Club: Lawn Tennis Club

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). Each player uses a tennis racket that is strung with cord to strike a hollow rubber ball covered with felt over or around a net and into the opponent's court. Boys team secured Bronze and Girls team secured Gold medal IIIT Sports meet 2023.

Name of Club : Jazbaat- The Dramatics Society Club

Jazbaat-The Dramatics Society is the biggest cultural club of the institute which provides a platform to the students to explore all the areas of dramatics like stage plays, street plays, mono-acts, mimes etc. We are active throughout the year giving an amazing experience both to the club members and the audience. Club sessions are organized regularly and new avenues of drama are explored. Apart from acting we also work on areas like scripting, music (instrumental and vocal), sound and light management, stage and prop management etc. which is an integral part of drama. We have something to offer to everybody and this is the reason our family keeps growing every year and we attain new heights. After a successful year 2016-17 we have not just won but also received blissful respect and love from not only institutions in the state, but the nation-wide. We have metamorphosed into a dramatics society which has not just excelled in street plays but also in stage acts. We’ve had a constant winning streak, be it at THOMSO, the annual cultural fest of IIT Roorkee or CARPE DIEM, the annual cultural fest of IIM Calcutta or our very own Inter IIIT Techno-Cultural Fest hosted by our Institute. This year we are planning to go even bigger and better. It’s going to be a year to remember.

Name of Club: Astronomy & Physics Society Club

Faculty Mentor: Dr. Amit Viswakarma

To promote Amateur astronomy among the students that have joined the club so they have an interest in cosmos and if they want they can pursue their carrier in Astronomy/Astrophysics. To educate the members of the Astronomy Club about astronomy and the physics related to the heavenly bodies, and the law governing their movements and their formation and all the underlying principles.
