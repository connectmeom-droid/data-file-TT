About the Institute

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Kalyani (IIIT Kalyani) is one of the 20 premier IIITs established by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, under the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model. The institute came into existence through a tripartite collaboration among:

  • Government of India (50%)

  • Government of West Bengal (35%)

  • Industry Partners: Coal India Ltd., Rolta Foundation, and WEBEL (15%)

Recognized as an Institute of National Importance under the IIIT (PPP) Act, 2017, IIIT Kalyani is dedicated to shaping competent professionals in the field of Information Technology.

Academic Structure: Shaping Innovators of Tomorrow

The academic framework at IIIT Kalyani is thoughtfully crafted to transform young minds into competent engineers equipped to tackle both technological and societal challenges. Emphasizing curiosity, innovation, and social responsibility, our undergraduate programs aim to empower students to make meaningful contributions at the national and global levels.

Program Highlights

Comprehensive B.Tech. Programs
IIIT Kalyani offers dynamic and evolving undergraduate programs that foster academic excellence and practical skills.

Curriculum Aligned with Leading Institutions
Our curriculum draws inspiration from esteemed institutions such as IIT Kharagpur, IIT Kanpur, NIT Durgapur, IIIT Allahabad, IIIT Delhi, and IIIT Hyderabad. It is designed to address current and emerging industry demands.

Focus on Industry-Relevant Subjects
Students are introduced to modern and in-demand technologies including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Analytics, Blockchain, Internet of Things (IoT), Cryptography, Information Security, and Image Processing.

Interdisciplinary Approach
Courses are structured to promote interdisciplinary learning, with collaborative offerings across departments that encourage diverse viewpoints and holistic development.

Project-Based Learning
From the third year onwards, students engage in compulsory hands-on projects that offer real-world exposure in areas such as AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Analytics, and Computer Vision.

Flexible and Student-Centric Learning
A favorable student-teacher ratio and a credit-based academic structure enable students to progress at their own pace, supported by personalized mentorship and guidance.

Rules For Change Branch

IIIT Kalyani provides students with an opportunity to change their branch after the completion of the first semester, subject to merit and seat availability.

  • Eligibility: Based on the Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) obtained in the first semester

  • Limit: A maximum of 5% of students in each program are eligible for branch change

  • Vacancy: Branch change is allowed only if there are vacant seats in the desired program

  • Selection Criteria: Students are selected in descending order of CGPA among all applicants

This policy ensures a fair and transparent process, enabling students to realign their academic goals based on performance and interest.

Some of the esteemed institutions in Kalyani include:

  • Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Kalyani

These institutions are part of the Kalyani Innovation Network, a collaborative ecosystem aimed at nurturing entrepreneurship, research, and technological growth by connecting academia, industry, and government.
