About the Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology Senapati Manipur (IIIT Manipur)

Indian Institute of Information Technology Manipur (IIIT Manipur) was established by the act of parliament, government of India as an Institutes of National Importance and is functional since 2015 at Mantripukhri, imphal 795002 (near new secretariat). The Institute is governed by a Board of Governors (BoG) consisting of distinguished academicians, industry leaders and senior government officials. Additionally, IIT Guwahati was the mentor institution for IIIT Manipur. The institute is enabling Manipur to gear up for providing Information Technology related services as well as high-quality R&D in Information Technology.

IIIT Manipur offers the following 4 years B Tech programmes:

In the Department of Computer Science and Engineering:

- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) (4 years, Bachelor of Technology)

- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with specialization in Artificial Intelligence & data science (4 years, Bachelor of Technology)

- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with specialization in Cyber Security (4 years, Bachelor of Technology)

- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with specialization in Quantum Technologies (4 years, Bachelor of Technology)


In the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering:


- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) (4 years, Bachelor of Technology)

 - Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) with specialization in VLSI & Embedded Systems (4 years, Bachelor of Technology)


The PhD Programmes:


- Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

 - Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

- Ph.D. in Humanities and Basic Sciences (HBS)

The Institute is presently operational from its own City campus located at Mantripukhri, Imphal but has two campuses: one as City campus at Mantripukhri and the Main Campus at Mayangkhang. The Main campus which is under construction is of 150 acres and is at Mayangkhang. Expected to be inaugurated this year that is 2025. The campus is well equipped with smart classrooms, state-of-art laboratories for both CSE and ECE, computer center, conference hall, auditorium, playground, hostels, faculty quarters, guest house and own administrative offices. The excellent student-faculty ratio enables 360-degree technical supervision and personal development of each student.

The institute strives to be attentive to academic needs of every student and has been able to deliver 100% placement. Classes are conveniently scheduled between 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 PM. Institute-wide WiFi connectivity empowered students to explore about the life-changing tools 24*7.

The institute undertakes the mission to provide technical education by offering state-of-the-art undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs with international standards of excellence and with emphasis on research to undertake collaborative projects which offer opportunities for long-term interaction with academia and Information Technology (IT) industry as well as technology development in the area of national importance.

The institute has a vision of establishing itself as an Institution of excellence in AI, Data Science, Electronics, Cyber Security, Cyber Crime and Forensic so that human resource from IIIT Manipur will be prepared to face this growing global threat. It also envisages itself for achieving excellence in application of robotics facilitating human life to overcome congenital as well as acquired disabilities.

To contribute to Human Resource development of its neighboring countries, IIIT Manipur will be looking for opportunities to spread IT education to countries in South-East Asia by providing required facilities to students of that country to study in IIIT Manipur through bi-lateral agreements.

Rules For Change Branch

A student may opt for change from one program (branch) of studies to another after the completion of second semester. Change of branch/ programme shall be made strictly in order of merit of the applicants.

Criteria for selection:

1.    Candidates shall complete all the common credits required in the first two semesters of their studies with a CPI of 8.00 or more, in their first attempt.

2.    In case of a tie, the JEE Main rank of the applicants will be considered.

3.    The strength of a program (branch) should not fall below the existing strength by more than ten percent (10%) and should not go above the sanctioned strength by more than ten percent (10%).

Industry And Alumni Relations

The Industry-Institution Interaction Cell of IIIT Manipur in association with the R&D cell provides an excellent platform for both the students as well as the faculty members to be aware of industry expectations from the graduates. It serves as a podium to showcase the best practices, latest technologies in industry stand point and their implementation. The cell regularly conducts various workshops, invited lectures, industrial visits, training programs, competitions viz., hackathon etc. in collaboration with industries. The R&D cell promote various sponsored research activities. Faculty members and students conduct research in collaboration with reputed industries R&D organizations with funding from various agencies.

IIIT Manipur Alumni Association works to achieve its mission through membership, chapters and networks, student programs, advocacy, and special events. The association envisages for supporting a network of former graduates who will, in turn, help to raise the profile of IIIT Manipur.

Guiding Values:

To provide guidance to the present students in their endeavor for better employment and higher studies.

To promote the campus placement and internship through the old students working in reputed industries in India and abroad.

To arrange seminars, debates, workshops and also to arrange cultural and social welfare programs.

To get the valuable advises of the alumni in the overall development of the college.

Location and Accessibility

IIIT Manipur’s campus is within Imphal town on Imphal Kohima national highway, NH 2 and approximately 10 Km from Airport in Imphal. The campus is very secure, having New Secretariat on one side, CRPF camp on the other side and IT park opposite to the institute.

Imphal, capital of Manipur is well connected by air from various places of India. Various airlines operate daily flights to and from Imphal. Air India, Indigo and Air Asia are operating flights to Imphal either via Kolkata or Guwahati depending on origination of the flights.  Taxis and Auto rickshaws are readily available from the airport to the institute.

Rail route is yet to reach Imphal, but expected to reach Tupul near Imphal by 2023. At present nearest big Rail station is at Dimapur/guwahati and there are bus services to Imphal from Dimapur/guwahati. There are also bus services from its neighbouring cities like Kohima and Silchar.

Hotels of various categories including 5-star are available near IIIT campus in Imphal.
