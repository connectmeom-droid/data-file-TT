About the Institute

About IIIT Sri City

Indian Institute of Information Technology Sri City, Chittoor at Sri City, known as IIITS, is an Institute of National Importance set up under an Act of Parliament. The Institute is governed by a Board of Governors consisting of distinguished academicians, industry leaders and senior government officials. Additionally, IIIT Hyderabad and IIT Hyderabad were the mentor institutions for IIIT Sri City. In fact, the Institute follows a similar curriculum and pedagogy as IIIT Hyderabad.

Unique Location: The Campus spread across 81 acres and equipped with state-of-the-art Infrastructure in Sri City. Located 55 km north of Chennai on the Nellore Highway, Sri City boasts of seamless connectivity by road, air etc. (Visit: _http://www.sricity.in). IIITS is located in Sri City, a decade old state of the art industrial city spread over 8000 acres encompassing a multi-product Special Economic Zone (SEZ), Domestic Tariff Zone (DTZ), Free Trade & Warehousing Zone (FTWZ) and Electronics Manufacturing Cluster. Sri City is hosting over 180 large manufacturing MNCs from over 27 industrialized countries. The institute has access to the industries and social infrastructure available in Sri City through the Industry Partner.

Focus on Research: IIITS is a unique institution with a perfect blend of teaching and research. Focus on UG-led research aims to develop technology related research that can solve real-world problems and has an impact across the world and in India. The Institute has two research centers-Center for Smart Cities and MHRD funded Design and Innovation Center. _Currently, there are 10 ongoing external sponsored projects driven by our faculties; worth nearly 3 crore rupees. The sponsoring agencies include: Department of Science and Technology (DST), ISRO, Naval Research Board (NRB), etc.

Gyan Circle Ventures- TBI at IIIT Sri City:  Keeping up with the spirit of entrepreneurship, Gyan Circle Ventures serves as a hub for innovation and startups leading to development of new technologies and business opportunities as well as enable economic and social development in the region. Gyan Circle Ventures is funded by the Ministry of Information Technology (MeitY) under Technology Incubation and Development of Entrepreneurs (TIDE 2.0) scheme. IIIT Sri City has been part of MHRD Innovation Cell and established Institute Innovation Council. Furthermore, IIIT Sri City has established an Entrepreneurship Cell (E-cell) to support and enable students to create innovative ideas and convert them into business opportunities. E-cell trains students on how to be successful entrepreneurs with social responsibility.

B.Tech Curriculum

IIIT Sri City, Chittoor was mentored by IIIT Hyderabad in the initial years The curriculum at IIIT Sri City follows a practice-theory-practice approach wherein the students are first introduced to real-world engineering concepts by providing hands-on-training. This is followed by rigorous theory-based courses which are again followed up with hands-on courses with intense real-world projects, requiring intense involvement from each student. The curriculum has been designed keeping in mind the best practices of IIIT Hyderabad, IITs, and leading institutions of higher learning globally. The Institute combines top-class education with pioneering research, whereby undergraduate students are encouraged to participate in different research and technology development projects, starting from the 3rd year. 

IIIT Sri City offers B.Tech degree in three disciplines:

  1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) 

  2. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

  3. Artificial Intelligence and Data Science 

The primary objective of the B.Tech programme at IIIT Sri City is to prepare aspiring students having the potential to face challenges with industry-ready standards. The unique curriculum offered facilitates students in becoming top-notch engineers/entrepreneurs while also equipping them to pursue higher studies and research work. We give ample opportunity to learn both fundamentals of theory as well as its application aspects. The curriculum is designed on the foundation of concepts driven by real-world practices which are duly covered in the first half of the program. The second half is engaging in the sense that it provides avenues for developing special skills and gaining knowledge in various sub-areas, gives a hands-on approach through well-guided project works, and also provides the much-needed flexibility in selecting courses that suit individual students. Besides well-designed core subjects, Mathematics, Science, Humanities, and Skill Building Programs are an integral and interesting part of our distinctive curriculum. 

Unique Honors Programme:

This is a purely research-intensive part of the curriculum and the promising students meeting the eligibility criteria are encouraged to opt for the Honors Programme starting from the 5th semester and go on up to 8th semester. This Honors program has a set of defined deliverables like research papers, development of products and services, generation of IPs. Students failing in meeting these defined deliverables will get a plain B.Tech degree in the respective discipline.

 Unique Component in CSE Programme:

Full Stack Development (FSD) track focuses on producing graduates with industry ready skills in software development. This industry-focused FSD track consists of three courses delivered in a sequence such that the students learn and demonstrate the latest technologies, tools and libraries used in their job situations. These courses together make them learn all three components- Front end, back end and API/Services and be able to develop end to end applications involving all the components effectively. The track simultaneously focuses on making the student undertake a live project that helps them to implement those technology components learned across three courses. The choices of technologies, tools and libraries shall be based on the current industry needs and profile of the batch of students. Three courses offered in 4 to 6 Semesters under this Full-Stack Development track- Fundamentals of Full Stack Development, Framework Driven Front-End Development and Web Services and Backend Development.

Facilities

State-of-the-art Infrastructure

The Campus: A state-of-the-art campus is being built on 81 acres of beautiful terrain which is situated along a scenic perennial creek.  The campus is being designed and built according to international standards. The campus is ready and functional. IIIT Sri City is a residential institute with separate hostels for boys and girls with all necessary facilities, and it is mandatory for all students to stay on campus.  Students can access Campus-Network and computing facilities from their hostel rooms.  Two hostels for boys are ready and one hostel is functional specially for girls.  The construction for two more hostels and one dining hall is in progress.

Green Campus: Sri City where the IIIT is located is planned on Sustainability Principles. All off energy consumed by municipal services comes from Sri City Solar Farm. Sri City encourages its tenants to use a minimum of 40% of energy from renewable sources (Solar). Sri City also encourages electric vehicles for movement within Sri City. The IIIT- Sri City will endeavor to function on 100% Solar Energy from neighboring Solar Farms. It will also encourage all IIITians to use electrical vehicles for movement within the Campus. Most of the water requirement will be met through rainwater-harvesting and re-use of treated waste-water.

Labs: The Institute has air-conditioned, specialized, high-end equipment in each laboratory, as needed in research and development projects. The Institute has air-conditioned computer labs with sufficient work stations.

Networking: The institute is Wi-Fi enabled.  All buildings including the classrooms, hostels and the campus will be connected through a high-speed fiber-optic network.  Students can access Campus-Network and computing facilities from their laptops and computers anywhere in the campus.

Library: The Library has a good  collection of reference books, technical reports, journals, and general books.  The institute subscribes to all leading journals/magazines. The library is open on all days. In addition, the Institute provides access to several digital library platforms like Swayam, NPTEL, E-Vidwan, National Digital Library (NDL), etc.

Student Life at Institute

Hostel Facilities: IIIT is a residential Institute and it is compulsory for all students to stay on-campus. Separate hostels for boys and girls are available with necessary facilities. Students can access Campus-Network and computing facilities from their hostel rooms.

Dining Facilities: The Institute has multiple messes serving a choice of healthy and nutritious South Indian and North Indian food.  Importance is given to maintain a hygienic environment in the messes.  There is a canteen which serves snacks, non-veg food, general fast foods, juices, milk products. The mess is managed by a committee of Students, Staff and Faculty.

Sports: The institute encourages students to actively participate in at least one sporting activity based on his/her individual aptitude. The institute provides sports facilities and equipment for a variety of sports such as cricket, football, basket-ball, badminton, volleyball etc. There are tournaments held on a regular basis that ensure a healthy, active atmosphere among students.

Gym: Gym facilities for men & women with modern equipment  are available in hostels.

Summer Internship

Students at IIIT Sri City are highly encouraged to seek internships during summer irrespective of which year they are enrolled in the institute.

While it may sound quite aspirational that we are encouraging even the UG first-year students to seek internships, we believe that an industry experience (from the first year) will go a long way in helping a student launch a bright career, from our institute. So, we encourage students as soon as they join to even get work-from-home internships. We feel that every single aspect of learning should be implemented rigorously and executed with due diligence. Internships are one such milestone at the IIITS.

Of course, our focus is to get the pre-final year students to get summer internships, as there is a good chance that these internships can get converted to Pre-Placement Offers (PPO). Google, Microsoft,Amazon, American Express, Yahoo, Swiggy, ZS Associates, Bosch, Commvault are a few of them where our students are currently indulged in their high-end lead projects.

Students are also encouraged to take academic internship opportunities both here in India and abroad. The placement office also brings such opportunities to students so that they can apply for many opportunities.

Semester-Long Project:

Semester-long project is one of the flagship programs of our institute and we have brought in novel aspects into this program so that it benefits the students. There are at least two ways that students benefit from these projects:

Students who aspire for their dream jobs sign-up with companies for the program of a Semester-long project with an opportunity to convert into a full-time position.

Often the companies visiting the campus for recruitment also offer students an opportunity to join as interns before the students join in the full-time positions in the same company. So, students have a gradual transition from students to their full-time roles.

The students get eight credits for these projects, and we have a rigorous review. The students who had opted for a Semester-long project only will be evaluated twice by the industry mentor and the institute faculty mentor.

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, a flagship programme of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) , Govt. of India, to enable participating higher educational institutions to work with people of rural India in identifying the development challenges and evolving appropriate solutions for accelerating sustainable growth. As per the programme, educational institutions are primarily to develop linkage with selective rural clusters (preferably of five villages).

NSS Cell
The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of the Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It provides opportunity to the student youth of Technical Institution of India to take part in various government led community service activities & programmes. The sole aim of the NSS is to provide hands on experience to young students in delivering community service. Since inception of the NSS in the year 1969, the number of students’ strength increased from 40,000 to over 4 million from different universities, colleges and Institutions of higher learning have volunteered to take part in varied community service programmes.

Location and Accessibility

IIITS - Part of Extended Greater Chennai: IIITS is located 60 KMs from Chennai on the Nellore Highway (NH16), connected through Inner and Outer ring roads. The institute is operating an office in Chennai for effective linkages with the Chennai industrial and entrepreneurship ecosystem.

IIITS- Close Proximity to Tirupati: Tirupati is about 70 KMs from Sri City. Apart from being a world-famous temple city, Tirupati is also a fast developing city. Notably, in Tirupati, a complete ecosystem of institutions such as IIT, ISSER, etc has been set-up along with electronic manufacturing clusters (EMC). IIIT Sri City intends to work with these clusters, especially in setting up electronic-based start-ups in the institute.

IIITS - Integral Part of Sri City: IIITS is located as an integral part of Sri City, a large integrated business city with state of the art industrial ecosystem on the Chennai-Nellore Highway. This decade-old state of the art industrial city spread over 8000 acres encompassing a multi-product Special Economic Zone (SEZ), Domestic Tariff Zone (DTZ), Free Trade & Warehousing Zone (FTWZ) and Electronics Manufacturing Cluster. Sri City which hosts more than 180 large manufacturing MNCs from over 27 industrialized countries. These companies are from a variety of fields including automotive, electronics, consumer goods, etc. Sri City MNCs include Alstom, Isuzu, Kellogg’s, Colgate-Palmolive, Kobelco, PepsiCo, Mondelez, etc. which have adopted smart manufacturing systems. IIITS has valuable collaboration with these companies, enabled through the Industry Partner Sri City Private Ltd. Visit: http://www.sricity.in.

The institute is surrounded by Mountains, Waterfalls, Birds Sanctuaries, Small Islands etc., thus providing opportunities for recreational activities. Sri City also hosts two hotels- Mango Resorts and The Inn as part of the social infrastructure. Given the presence of multinational companies, people from other countries are living in and around Sri City. Several Apartment Hotels along with international cuisines are in the immediate vicinity.

The new age KREA university with Liberal Arts focus and its Business School are in Sri City. Satellite Launching Station of Indian Space Research Organization is located near Sri City. The Satish Dhawan Space Centre (aka SHAR), India’s Satellite/Rocket launching center is located at Sriharikota, on the eastern side of the Pulicat Lake which separates Sri City and SHAR.
