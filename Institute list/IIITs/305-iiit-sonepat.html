About the Institute

Those who are allotted IIIT Sonepat , they are requested to report to the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Sonepat, SBIT Campus, Meerut Road, Pallri, Sonepat-131001


Sonepat is a railway Station on Delhi-Karnal-Ambala section of Northern Railway.  It is about 60 KM by road from Delhi on National Highway-44 .  The Institute campus is about 10 Km on Meerut Road from Bahalgarh Sonepat, a well known road junction on NH-44.

For more details, one may visit: www.iiitsonepat.ac.in
