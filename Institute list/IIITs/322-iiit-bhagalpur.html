About the Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhagalpur (IIIT Bhagalpur) is one of the IIITs set up by MHRD, Govt of India in PUBLIC-PRIVATE PARTNERSHIP(PPP) mode. It started functioning from the academic year 2017.

Indian Institute of Information Technology Bhagalpur is a joint venture of Govt. of India (50%), Govt. of Bihar (35%) and BELTRON (15%). The Institute started its first academic session from 1st August 2017. Govt of Bihar has provided 50 acres of land to IIIT Bhagalpur carved out of BCE Bhagalpur campus on the banks of river Ganga. Since then, IIIT Bhagalpur is on a fascinating journey of growth & development. In terms of its impact as a technical institution in the country, the institute has already made some significant achievements. The permanent campus of the Institute having state of art facilities is operational since April 2024.

Currently, the institute has B. Tech programs in Four branches namely Computer Science Engineering (150 seats) and Electronics and Communication Engineering (75 seats), Mechatronics and Automation Engineering (60 seats), Mathematics and Computing (27 Seats). The admission of the students is held through JEE MAINS organized by JOSAA/CSAB.

The Institute started PhD and M.Tech programs in various programs from 2021.
